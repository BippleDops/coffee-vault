Grind Size Guide

Extra Coarse (Cold Brew)

  • Looks like: Sea salt or peppercorns
  • Brew time: 12-24 hours
  • Methods: Cold brew, cowboy coffee

Coarse (French Press)

  • Looks like: Kosher salt
  • Brew time: 4 minutes
  • Methods: French press, percolator

Medium-Coarse (Chemex)

  • Looks like: Rough sand
  • Brew time: 3-4 minutes
  • Methods: Chemex, Clever dripper

Medium (Drip Coffee)

  • Looks like: Regular sand
  • Brew time: 3-4 minutes
  • Methods: Drip, pour over, Aeropress

Medium-Fine (V60)

  • Looks like: Fine sand or table salt
  • Brew time: 2-3 minutes
  • Methods: V60, Kalita Wave, cone filters

Fine (Espresso)

  • Looks like: Powdered sugar
  • Brew time: 25-30 seconds
  • Methods: Espresso, Moka pot

Flavor Profile Analysis

Acidity
Brightness, liveliness. High acidity = crisp, vibrant. Low acidity = smooth, mellow.
Sweetness
Natural sugars, caramel notes. High sweetness = rich, balanced. Low sweetness = dry, savory.
Bitterness
Dark chocolate, roast character. High bitterness = bold, intense. Low bitterness = delicate, tea-like.
Body
Mouthfeel, texture. High body = heavy, creamy. Low body = light, clean.

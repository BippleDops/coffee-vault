## üìã Overview

Ethiopia Heirloom Kurume is one of the identified Ethiopian landrace varietals - part of Ethiopia's thousands of indigenous coffee genetics that create the country's unmatched flavor diversity. Kurume produces exceptionally floral, bergamot-forward coffees with tea-like delicacy.

**Characteristics**:
- **Ethiopian Landrace**: Indigenous wild-type variety
- **Floral-Forward**: Intense florals, bergamot, tea-like
- **Genetic Diversity**: Part of Ethiopia's biodiversity treasure
- **Rarely Labeled Specifically**: Usually sold as "Ethiopian Heirloom" (mixed varietals)
- **When Separated**: Exceptional quality, distinctive profile
- **Research**: Ongoing variety identification research in Ethiopia

**What "Heirloom" Means**:
- Ethiopia has thousands of wild indigenous varieties
- Most unlabeled, sold as "heirloom" or "indigenous varieties"
- Kurume is one identified/characterized variety
- Represents Ethiopia's genetic diversity

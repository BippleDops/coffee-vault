## üìã Overview

Kenya K7 is traditional Kenyan Bourbon selection - selected from French Mission Bourbon for quality and adaptability, producing berry-citrus brightness with fuller body and balanced sweetness that makes it more approachable than extreme SL-28/34 profiles. While overshadowed by SL varieties, K7 delivers excellent Kenyan quality with broader appeal.

**Characteristics**:
- **French Mission Bourbon**: Selected from colonial-era Bourbon plantings
- **Berry-Citrus**: Kenyan signature brightness maintained
- **Fuller Body**: Heavier than SL varieties (more balanced)
- **Less Extreme**: Not as intensely acidic as SL-28 (more approachable)
- **Quality**: Excellent (85-88 points achievable)
- **Declining**: Being replaced by SL and Batian varieties
- **Specialty**: Maintained on quality-focused farms

**Development**:
- **1930s-1940s**: Selected from French Mission Bourbon
- **Selection**: K series (K for Kenya) selection program
- **Adoption**: Common alongside SL varieties historically
- **Present**: ~10-15% production, declining but valued

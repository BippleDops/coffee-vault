## üìã Overview

Ethiopia 74110 is one of the Jimma Agricultural Research Center's improved varieties - selected from Ethiopian landrace genetics for productivity, disease resistance, and quality, representing Ethiopia's effort to combine wild genetic diversity with agricultural reliability. While less famous than pure heirloom lots, 74110 delivers balanced Ethiopian character with better farm economics.

**What is 74110**:
- **Research Selection**: Jimma Agricultural Research Center (JARC) breeding program
- **Ethiopian Genetics**: Selected from local landrace populations (not hybrid)
- **Improved**: Selected for yield, disease resistance, cup quality
- **Number**: Selection number from research program (74 series)
- **1970s Release**: Distributed to farmers 1970s-1980s
- **Widely Planted**: 10-20% of Ethiopian coffee includes 74110
- **Better Economics**: Higher yield than pure wild landrace

**74 Series Varieties**:
- **74110**: Most common, balanced profile
- **74112**: Similar, sister selection
- **74140, 74148, 74165**: Other JARC selections
- **Goal**: Improve farmer productivity while maintaining quality

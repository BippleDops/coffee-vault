## üìã Overview

Ethiopia Harrar (also spelled Harar) is one of coffee's most historic and polarizing origins - exclusively dry-processed (natural) coffee from eastern Ethiopia's ancient Islamic city, producing intensely fruity, wine-like, sometimes funky coffees with wild blueberry character that divides coffee lovers into passionate devotees and complete avoiders.

**What Makes Harrar Unique**:
- **Natural Process Only**: NO washed coffee from Harrar (dry climate, tradition)
- **Wild Blueberry**: Signature flavor - intense, recognizable, beloved or hated
- **Funky**: "Harrar Funk" - earthy, fermented, wine-like notes (polarizing)
- **Historic**: One of world's oldest coffee-producing regions
- **Dry Climate**: Eastern highlands, arid, ideal for natural processing
- **Full Body**: Very full-bodied, syrupy, coating mouthfeel
- **Traditional**: Ancient processing methods preserved
- **Variability**: Lot-to-lot variation higher than washed Ethiopian coffees
- **Polarizing**: Extreme reactions - love intensely or dislike strongly

**The "Harrar Funk"**:
- **Descriptor**: Earthy, fermented, wine-like, sometimes barnyard notes
- **Source**: Natural processing in dry climate, specific fermentation/drying
- **Division**: 
  - **Fans**: Find it complex, interesting, terroir expression, desirable
  - **Critics**: Find it defect, off-flavor, unclean, unpleasant
- **Reality**: Characteristic of Harrar, not defect (when done well)
- **Spectrum**: Ranges from subtle to intense depending on lot

## ‚òï Flavor Profile

**Natural Harrar (Light-Medium Roast)**:

**Primary Flavors**:
- **Blueberry**: Wild blueberry, blueberry jam, intense
- **Berry**: Blackberry, raspberry, mixed berries, fruit compote
- **Wine**: Red wine, fermented fruit, grape, wine-like complexity
- **Chocolate**: Dark chocolate, cocoa (develops with roast)
- **Funk**: Earthy, fermented, barnyard, forest floor (varies by lot)

**Sensory**:
- **Acidity**: Medium - Fruit-driven, not bright like washed Ethiopian
- **Body**: Full to Very Full - Syrupy, coating, heavy, viscous
- **Sweetness**: High - Berry jam, fruit sugars, chocolate
- **Complexity**: High - Layered fruit, wine, earth, chocolate
- **Funky**: Variable - some lots clean, some funky
- **Finish**: Long - Berry-wine notes persist

**Comparison to Other Ethiopian**:
- **Bolder**: Than Yirgacheffe (less delicate)
- **Fuller Body**: Than washed Ethiopian
- **Funkier**: Than Sidamo (more fermented notes)
- **Lower Acidity**: Than washed processed
- **More Polarizing**: Extreme reactions common

## üìã Overview

Kenya Ruiru 11 is Kenya's practical response to coffee berry disease and rust - a resistant hybrid enabling Kenyan farmers to continue producing coffee economically. While not matching SL-28/SL-34's legendary complexity, Ruiru 11 maintains good cup quality with disease protection, representing Kenyan coffee's sustainable future.

**Characteristics**:
- **Disease Resistant**: Coffee berry disease + rust resistance
- **Productive**: Higher yield than SL varieties
- **Good Quality**: Maintains Kenyan brightness (not exceptional complexity)
- **Compact**: Smaller plants, easier management
- **Adoption**: 20-30% of Kenyan coffee, growing
- **Economic**: Enables sustainable Kenyan coffee farming

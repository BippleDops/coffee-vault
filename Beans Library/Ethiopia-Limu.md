## üìã Overview

Ethiopia Limu is western Ethiopia's balanced specialty - washed-processed coffee from Jimma Zone producing wine-like, spice-forward, balanced profiles that bridge Yirgacheffe's delicate florals and Sidamo's berry intensity. Limu represents Ethiopian coffee's versatile middle ground: complex yet approachable, distinctive yet balanced.

**What Makes Limu Notable**:
- **Western Ethiopia**: Different terroir from famous eastern regions
- **Washed Processing**: Almost exclusively washed (creates balanced profiles)
- **Wine-Like**: Signature wine-grape character, vinous complexity
- **Spice Notes**: Sweet spice prominent (cinnamon, clove, cardamom)
- **Balanced**: More balanced than extreme Yirgacheffe or funky Harrar
- **Reliable**: Consistent quality, good for blending
- **Underrated**: Less famous than Yirgacheffe/Sidamo but excellent quality
- **Value**: Good Ethiopian quality at accessible pricing

**Historical Context**:
- **Traditional Region**: Historic coffee-producing area
- **Commercial**: Larger production than boutique regions
- **Reliable**: Consistent quality year-over-year
- **Export**: Significant export volumes (vs. micro-regions)

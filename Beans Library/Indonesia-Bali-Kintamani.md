## üìã Overview

Bali Kintamani is Indonesian coffee's bright exception - grown in volcanic highlands of touristheavy Bali, producing citrus-forward, floral, surprisingly bright coffees that defy Indonesian stereotypes. Unlike typical earthy Sumatra, Bali coffee showcases volcanic terroir with clean, spice-forward profiles enhanced by unique cultural context.

**What Makes Kintamani Unique**:
- **Bright Indonesian**: Citrus-spice vs. typical earthy-herbal
- **Volcanic**: Mount Batur and Agung volcanic influence
- **Washed Available**: More washed processing than Sumatra
- **Geographic Indication**: Protected PDO status
- **Tourism**: Coffee agrotourism in Bali
- **Different**: Doesn't taste like typical Indonesian
- **Clean**: When washed, very clean profiles

**Kintamani PDO** (Protected Designation of Origin):
- UNESCO recognition
- Specific geographic boundaries
- Quality standards
- Cultural protection

## üìã Overview

Brazil Red Bourbon represents traditional Brazilian quality - standard red-cherry Bourbon producing sweet, chocolate-forward, full-bodied coffees that defined Brazilian specialty before Yellow Bourbon gained fame. While less celebrated than its yellow sibling, Red Bourbon delivers reliable excellence with classic Brazilian character.

**Characteristics**:
- **Traditional Bourbon**: Standard red-cherry variety
- **Sweet**: Exceptional sweetness, Bourbon hallmark
- **Full Body**: Syrupy, creamy, smooth Brazilian signature
- **Low Acid**: Gentle, mellow, stomach-friendly
- **Natural Processing**: Brazilian natural process amplifies sweetness
- **Reliable**: Consistent quality, predictable profiles
- **Value**: Excellent quality-to-price ratio

**vs. Yellow Bourbon**:
- **Similar Quality**: Both excellent, personal preference
- **Slightly Less Sweet**: Yellow Bourbon marginally sweeter (subjective)
- **More Common**: Red Bourbon more widely available
- **Lower Price**: Often 10-20% less than Yellow (unjustified by quality)
- **Equal**: Cup quality comparable, color main difference

## üå± F1 Hybrid Science

**What is F1**:
- **First Generation**: Cross of two distinct parent lines
- **Heterosis**: Hybrid vigor (offspring superior to either parent)
- **Uniform**: All F1 plants identical (clonal uniformity)
- **Can't Save Seed**: F2 generation loses uniformity (must buy new seed)
- **Like Hybrid Corn**: Agricultural model proven in other crops

**Centroamericano Parents**:
- **Complex Genetics**: Proprietary parent lines
- **Selected For**: Quality, yield, resistance, climate adaptation
- **Result**: Best-of-all-worlds variety

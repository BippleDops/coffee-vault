## üìã Overview

Costa Rica Catuai brings Brazilian productive hybrid to Costa Rican legendary processing - producing balanced, honey-sweet, exceptionally clean coffees that combine Catuai's reliability with Costa Rican terroir excellence. As one of Costa Rica's most common varieties (30-40% of production), Catuai provides consistent quality that maintains Costa Rica's reputation.

**Characteristics**:
- **Productive**: 40-60% better yield than Bourbon
- **Costa Rican Processing**: Legendary washed/honey processing creates exceptional quality
- **Honey-Sweet**: Costa Rican terroir + Catuai = signature sweetness
- **Balanced**: Chocolate-citrus-honey harmony
- **Wind-Resistant**: Important for exposed Central Valley plantations
- **Reliable**: Consistent quality, forgiving, versatile
- **Common**: 30-40% of Costa Rican coffee
- **Value**: Excellent quality-to-price ratio

**Why Widely Adopted in Costa Rica**:
- Balances productivity and quality
- Performs well across altitude range
- Works with both washed and honey processing
- Wind-resistant (critical for Central Valley)
- Market acceptance (buyers recognize quality)

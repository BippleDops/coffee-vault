## üìã Overview

Java Jampit Estate represents Indonesian coffee's washed excellence - heritage colonial-era estate producing clean, balanced, chocolate-spice coffees through fully washed processing that showcases what Indonesian terroir offers without wet-hulling's earthy character. Jampit proves Indonesian coffee can be clean, bright, and complex.

**What Makes Jampit Special**:
- **Heritage Estate**: Colonial Dutch-era estate (1800s), continuous operation
- **Washed Processing**: Fully washed (rare for Indonesia), estate control
- **Clean Indonesian**: Shows Indonesian terroir without extreme earthiness
- **Quality Control**: Estate processing ensures consistency
- **Chocolate-Spice**: Balanced, complex, unique
- **Value**: Good Indonesian quality at reasonable pricing
- **Different**: Not typical wet-hulled Indonesian profile

**Jampit Estate History**:
- **Dutch Colonial**: Established during Dutch East India Company era
- **Continuous Operation**: 150+ years coffee production

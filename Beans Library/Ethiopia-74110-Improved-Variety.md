## üìã Overview

Ethiopia 74110 is a selected Ethiopian variety developed by Jimma Agricultural Research Center - combining Ethiopia's landrace genetics with improved productivity and disease resistance while maintaining Ethiopian flavor complexity. It represents Ethiopia's agricultural research success: quality preservation with practical farming benefits.

**What is 74110**:
- **Improved Selection**: From Ethiopian heirloom/landrace genetics
- **Jimma Research**: Ethiopian government research center selection
- **Better Yield**: 30-40% more productive than pure landraces
- **Disease Resistance**: Improved resistance to coffee berry disease
- **Quality**: Maintains Ethiopian character (floral, berry, complexity)
- **Balanced**: More balanced than extreme Ethiopian profiles
- **Adoption**: Growing among farmers needing productivity

**74110 and 74112**:
- **Siblings**: Both from same Jimma breeding program
- **74110**: More common, widely planted
- **74112**: Similar characteristics, less common
- **Purpose**: Enable Ethiopian farmers to compete economically while preserving quality

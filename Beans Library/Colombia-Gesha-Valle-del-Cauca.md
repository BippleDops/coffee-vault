## üìã Overview

Colombia Gesha represents Geisha variety's Colombian expression - high-altitude Andean terroir meeting Ethiopian genetics to create jasmine-tropical complexity with Colombian processing refinement. While less famous than Panama Geisha, Colombian Gesha delivers exceptional quality at more accessible pricing while showcasing how terroir transforms this celebrated variety.

**Why Colombian Gesha Matters**:
- **Unique Terroir**: Colombian volcanic Andean expression (different from Panama)
- **High Altitude**: 1,700-2,100 MASL ideal for Geisha
- **Processing Excellence**: Colombian washed expertise + experimental anaerobic
- **More Affordable**: $25-60/lb vs. Panama $100-1,000/lb
- **Competition**: Colombian Gesha wins awards
- **Growing**: Multiple farms now producing Geisha
- **Future**: Colombia becoming important Geisha origin beyond Panama

## üìã Overview

Honduras IHCAFE 90 is high-productivity rust-resistant variety developed for commodity and commercial production - delivering reliable chocolate-nut profiles with full body but lower complexity than traditional varieties. It represents agricultural pragmatism: disease resistance and yield prioritized over ultimate cup quality.

**Characteristics**:
- **Very Productive**: 60-80% higher yield than Bourbon/Caturra
- **Rust Resistant**: Strong disease resistance
- **Lower Quality**: Cup quality below Lempira, Bourbon, traditional varieties
- **Commodity Focus**: Primarily commercial/commodity production
- **Declining in Specialty**: Specialty farmers prefer Lempira, Parainema
- **Still Common**: 15-25% of Honduran coffee (commodity sector)

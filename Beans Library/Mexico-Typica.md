## üìã Overview

Mexico Typica represents Mexican coffee's traditional heritage - original variety brought centuries ago, maintained by organic smallholder farmers in shade-grown systems, producing balanced, chocolate-nut coffees with exceptional sustainability credentials. As Mexico's quality variety, Typica delivers gentle, approachable profiles perfect for daily drinking with environmental and social consciousness.

**Why Mexico Typica Matters**:
- **Heritage**: Original variety in Mexico (1700s-1800s introduction)
- **Organic Leader**: Mexico's organic production (25%+ global leader) includes significant Typica
- **Shade-Grown**: Traditional agroforestry systems preserve biodiversity
- **Bird-Friendly**: Smithsonian certified shade systems common
- **Low-Yield Maintained**: Quality and sustainability premiums justify Typica
- **Gentle Profile**: Accessible, mild, balanced - broad appeal
- **Ethical Coffee**: Triple certification (organic, fair trade, bird-friendly) common

**Traditional Mexican Coffee**:
- Typica brought via Caribbean (1700s)
- Became established in southern states
- Shade-grown under native forest trees (traditional)
- Organic by practice (smallholders, indigenous communities)
- Certifications formalized existing practices (1990s-2000s)

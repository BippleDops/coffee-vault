- **Origin**: Natural Bourbon mutation discovered in El Salvador (1949)
- **Discovery**: Pacas family farm (hence "Pacas" name)
- **Characteristics**: Compact, productive, quality maintained
- **Spread**: Adopted in Honduras, Nicaragua, other Central American countries

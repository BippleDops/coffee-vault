## üìã Overview

Tanzania Kent is unique East African coffee - Indian-origin rust-resistant variety (introduced 1920s) adapted to Tanzanian volcanic terroir, producing berry-citrus brightness with fuller body and balanced character that bridges extreme Kenyan acidity and approachable drinking.

**Why Kent is Special in Tanzania**:
- **Rust Resistance**: Brought from India specifically for resistance (1920s colonial era)
- **Heritage**: 100 years in Tanzania, adapted to Mount Kilimanjaro/Meru terroir
- **Quality**: Maintains East African brightness with balanced body
- **Unique to East Africa**: Only Tanzania has significant Kent production in region
- **Fuller Body**: Than Kenya SL varieties, more substantial
- **Balanced Acidity**: Bright but not extreme (more approachable than Kenya)
- **Reliable**: Consistent quality, good productivity
- **Value**: Excellent quality-to-price ratio

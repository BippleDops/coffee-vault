## üìã Overview

Honduras Lempira is modern coffee breeding success - Honduran-developed rust-resistant variety that maintains specialty-grade cup quality while providing disease protection, proving productivity and quality can coexist. Lempira represents Honduras' response to coffee rust crisis and commitment to quality specialty coffee future.

**What Makes Lempira Important**:
- **Honduran Innovation**: Bred by IHCAFE specifically for Honduran conditions
- **Rust Resistant**: Strong Hemileia vastatrix (rust) resistance
- **Quality Maintained**: Better cup quality than typical rust-resistant varieties
- **Productive**: High yield enables economic viability
- **Government Support**: Heavily promoted as rust replacement
- **Specialty-Grade**: Can achieve 83-86+ cup scores
- **Rapid Adoption**: 20-25% of Honduran coffee (growing)
- **Success Model**: Shows rust resistance doesn't require quality sacrifice

**Development**:
- **Parent**: Sarchimor (Villa Sarchi √ó Timor Hybrid)
- **IHCAFE**: Instituto Hondure√±o del Caf√© research and breeding
- **Released**: 2000s for farmer distribution
- **Rust Crisis** (2012-2014): Accelerated adoption dramatically
- **Present**: Second or third most common Honduran variety
- **Future**: Likely to continue growing (economics + quality)

**Lempira vs. Other Rust-Resistant Varieties**:
- **Better than Catimor/IHCAFE 90**: Noticeably superior cup quality
- **Comparable to Castillo** (Colombian): Similar quality-resistance balance
- **Not quite Bourbon/Caturra**: Still lower complexity than traditional varieties
- **Acceptable for Specialty**: Quality adequate for specialty market

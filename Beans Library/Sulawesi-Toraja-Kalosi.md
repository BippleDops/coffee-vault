## üìã Overview

Sulawesi Toraja Kalosi is Indonesian coffee's refined expression - from Tana Toraja's Kalosi district producing balanced, chocolate-spice profiles with less extreme earthiness than Sumatra. Kalosi represents Indonesian specialty at its most approachable: maintaining full body and low acidity while offering cleaner, more balanced flavors.

**Characteristics**:
- **Kalosi District**: Specific area within Tana Toraja (quality designation)
- **Higher Altitude**: 1,200-1,800 MASL (higher than most Sumatra)
- **Balanced Indonesian**: Less earthy than Sumatra, more approachable
- **Chocolate-Spice**: Sweet chocolate, spice notes prominent
- **Washed Option**: More washed processing than Sumatra (cleaner cups available)
- **Cultural**: Toraja people's coffee traditions
- **Value**: Excellent Indonesian quality at reasonable pricing

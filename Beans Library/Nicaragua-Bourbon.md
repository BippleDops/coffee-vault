## üìã Overview

Nicaragua Bourbon delivers traditional variety excellence through Nicaraguan volcanic terroir - producing sweet, balanced, chocolate-fruit coffees with exceptional complexity that showcase why Nicaragua chose quality-focused specialty strategy. Alongside Maracaturra and Pacamara, Bourbon forms Nicaragua's quality foundation.

**Characteristics**:
- **Traditional**: Heritage variety, pre-war plantings maintained
- **Exceptional Sweetness**: Bourbon + Nicaraguan terroir = remarkable sweetness
- **Balanced**: Chocolate-fruit-caramel harmony
- **Complex**: Layered, multi-dimensional, rewarding
- **Honey Processing**: Nicaraguan honey process innovation amplifies Bourbon sweetness
- **Competition**: Bourbon lots win or place regularly
- **Value**: Excellent quality-to-price for traditional variety

**Nicaragua's Bourbon Heritage**:
- **Pre-1980s**: Dominant variety in Nicaragua
- **War Impact** (1980s): Coffee industry devastated
- **Post-War**: Some Bourbon maintained (couldn't afford replacement)
- **Specialty Era**: Bourbon valued for quality (not liability)
- **Present**: ~10-20% production, valued for complexity
- **Premium**: Commands premiums over Catuai/Catimor

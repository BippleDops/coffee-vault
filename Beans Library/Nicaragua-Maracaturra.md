## üìã Overview

Nicaragua Maracaturra is one of coffee's most distinctive varieties - combining giant Maragogipe beans with Caturra's quality to create enormous beans (screen size 18-22+) that produce intensely fruity, floral, sweet coffees with unique flavor profiles that have become Nicaragua's calling card in the specialty market. While agricultural challenges limit production, Maracaturra's exceptional cup quality and visual drama make it a specialty darling commanding premium prices.

**What Makes Maracaturra Extraordinary**:
- **Giant Beans**: Largest coffee beans in commercial production (with Pacamara)
- **Nicaragua's Signature**: Synonymous with Nicaraguan specialty coffee globally
- **Extreme Fruit**: Tropical fruit, berry, floral - intense and memorable
- **Visual Impact**: Beans 50-100% larger than standard, dramatically impressive
- **Low Yield**: Very low productivity (25-40% of normal varieties)
- **Competition Winner**: Cup of Excellence Nicaragua regular winner
- **Premium**: Commands 30-100% premiums over standard Nicaraguan coffee
- **Unique**: Flavor profile unlike any other coffee (instantly recognizable)
- **Polarizing**: Intense flavors - love it or find it overwhelming

**Development**:
- **Parents**: 
  - **Maragogipe**: Brazilian Typica mutation, elephant beans, unique flavor
  - **Caturra**: Bourbon mutation, compact, quality, productive
- **Cross**: Maragogipe √ó Caturra = Maracaturra
- **Goal**: Combine large bean size with reasonable productivity
- **Reality**: Got size and flavor, NOT high productivity
- **Adoption**: Nicaragua embraced it (1990s-2000s), became national specialty
- **Global**: Grown elsewhere but performs best in Nicaragua

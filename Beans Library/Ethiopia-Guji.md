## üìã Overview

Ethiopia Guji represents Ethiopian specialty coffee's newest star - a high-altitude zone separated from Sidama in 2010 that has rapidly gained recognition for exceptional quality, distinctive peach-apricot character, and some of Ethiopia's highest-altitude coffees. Guji combines Yirgacheffe's complexity with Sidamo's body and its own unique stone fruit signature.

**What Makes Guji Exceptional**:
- **Very High Altitude**: 1,800-2,300 MASL (among Ethiopia's highest)
- **Peach-Apricot Signature**: Distinctive stone fruit character
- **Recent Recognition**: Separated as own designation 2010, rapidly gained fame
- **Exceptional Sweetness**: Extremely sweet, fruit-driven
- **Complex**: Multi-dimensional, layered, evolving
- **Both Processes Excel**: Washed and natural both produce excellence
- **Competition Winner**: Cup of Excellence regular, 88-92+ points
- **Growing Reputation**: Quickly becoming as recognized as Yirgacheffe, Sidamo

**The Guji Story**:
- **Pre-2010**: Part of Sidama designation
- **2010**: Separated as independent zone (political reorganization)
- **2010-2015**: Specialty market discovers distinct Guji character
- **2015-Present**: Rapid recognition growth, premium pricing
- **Quality Recognition**: Guji now considered equal to Yirgacheffe for quality

### Washed Guji ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

**Process**: Traditional Ethiopian washed
1. Selective picking
2. Depulping (same day)
3. Fermentation: 24-48 hours
4. Washing: Clean water

**Flavor Profile**:

- **Berry**: Blueberry, strawberry, mixed berries

- **Honey**: Floral honey, sweet
- **Body**: Medium to Medium-Full - Silky, juicy, coating
- **Acidity**: High - Vibrant, citric, malic, complex
- **Sweetness**: Very High - Stone fruit sugars, honey, exceptional
- **Complexity**: Extreme - Layered, evolving, multi-dimensional
- **Clean**: Crystal clear cup, zero defects
- **Finish**: Very Long - Stone fruit and floral persist

**Character**: Bright, clean, complex, stone-fruit dominant, exceptionally sweet

### Natural Guji ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

**Process**: Whole cherry dried on raised beds, 20-30 days

**Flavor Profile**:
- **Berry Bomb**: Blueberry, strawberry, blackberry, intense
- **Stone Fruit**: Peach, apricot (still present, less dominant than washed)
- **Tropical**: Pineapple, mango, passion fruit
- **Wine**: Red wine, fermented fruit, boozy, complex
- **Floral**: Perfumed, jasmine, intense florals
- **Body**: Full - Syrupy, viscous, coating, juicy
- **Acidity**: Medium-High - Fruit-driven rather than bright citric
- **Sweetness**: Extreme - Fruit sugars, berry jam, candy-like
- **Complexity**: Overwhelming - Multiple fruit layers
- **Finish**: Very Long - Berry and fruit persistence

**Character**: Fruit-forward, syrupy, complex, sweet, intense

**Natural vs. Washed Guji**:
- **Washed**: Stone fruit (peach-apricot) signature, bright, clean, tea-like
- **Natural**: Berry-forward, fuller body, fruit bomb, syrupy
- **Both Excellent**: Preference-dependent, both win competitions

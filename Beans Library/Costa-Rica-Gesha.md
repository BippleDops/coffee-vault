Costa Rica Gesha combines Geisha variety's floral complexity with Costa Rican processing excellence - producing jasmine-forward, tropical-fruit coffees with legendary cleanliness and brightness. While Panama Geisha remains most famous, Costa Rican Gesha delivers exceptional quality at more accessible pricing ($30-60/lb vs. Panama's $100-1,000/lb).

- **Costa Rican Processing**: Legendary washed processing creates extreme clarity
- **Volcanic Terroir**: Enhances Geisha's natural complexity
- **Honey Processing Innovation**: Costa Rica's honey processing perfect for Geisha
- **More Affordable**: 50-70% less than Panama Geisha
- **Clean**: Exceptionally clean cup (Costa Rican signature)
- **Consistent**: Costa Rica's standards ensure quality consistency
- **Underrated**: Overshadowed by Panama but quality comparable

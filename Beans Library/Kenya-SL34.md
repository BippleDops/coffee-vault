## üìã Overview

Kenya SL-34 is SL-28's sibling variety - sharing the famous Kenyan blackcurrant character, bright acidity, and wine-like complexity, but with slightly sweeter profile, fuller body, and marginally lower altitude requirements. Together, SL-28 and SL-34 define Kenyan specialty coffee and have built Kenya's reputation as one of the world's premier origins.

**What Makes SL-34 Special**:
- **Sibling to SL-28**: From same breeding program, complementary characteristics
- **Blackcurrant**: Signature Kenyan flavor (Ribena, berry jam, cassis)
- **Sweet Bourbon Lineage**: Selected from French Mission Bourbon (sweeter than SL-28)
- **Full Body**: Fuller than SL-28, syrupy mouthfeel
- **Slightly Lower Acidity**: Still very bright, but touch less than SL-28
- **Altitude Flexible**: Performs well 1,400-2,000 MASL (SL-28 prefers 1,500-2,100)
- **Competition Winner**: Cup of Excellence regular
- **Blending**: Often blended with SL-28 for complexity + balance
- **Premium**: Commands top prices like SL-28

**SL-34 vs. SL-28**:
- **SL-34**: Sweeter, fuller body, slightly less acidic, French Mission Bourbon lineage
- **SL-28**: Brighter acidity, lighter body, more intense fruit, Tanganyika Drought-Resistant lineage
- **Reality**: Differences subtle, both excellent, often grown together
- **Blends**: Many Kenyan coffees are SL-28 + SL-34 blends (complementary)
- **Preference**: Personal taste determines favorite

## üå± The SL-34 Variety

**Development**:
- **1930s**: Scott Laboratories breeding program
- **Selection**: From **French Mission Bourbon** at Loresho Estate
- **Number**: Selection #34 from program
- **Characteristics**: Sweet, complex, full-bodied Bourbon type

**Genetics**:
- **Bourbon lineage**: Descended from French Mission Bourbon
- **Different from SL-28**: Different parent selection, distinct characteristics
- **Complementary**: Bred to complement SL-28

**Plant Traits**:
- **Height**: Tall (3-4 meters)
- **Leaves**: Bronze-tipped (Bourbon characteristic)
- **Yield**: Low to moderate (similar to SL-28)
- **Disease**: Coffee berry disease and rust susceptible
- **Drought**: Moderate tolerance

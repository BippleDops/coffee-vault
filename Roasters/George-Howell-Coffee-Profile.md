George Howell is specialty coffee's terroir pioneer - the roaster who introduced wine's "terroir" concept to coffee (1990s), proving coffee expresses geographic origin as profoundly as wine. From founding Coffee Connection (sold to Starbucks 1994) to George Howell Coffee (2004-present), Howell has championed quality, terroir, and light roasts for 50+ years.

**Why George Howell is Legendary**:
- **Terroir Concept**: First to systematically apply wine's terroir to coffee
- **Coffee Connection**: Founded 1974, pioneering specialty (sold to Starbucks 1994)
- **Quality Obsession**: Uncompromising standards, legendary cupper
- **Light Roasts**: Among first to champion light roasts (1980s-1990s)
- **Education**: Coffee education leader, terroir workshops
- **50+ Years**: Five decades of specialty coffee leadership
- **Respected**: Industry legend, universally respected

## üìã Overview

Intelligentsia Coffee is one of specialty coffee's foundational third-wave roasters - pioneering Direct Trade relationships, light roast origin-forward profiles, and barista championship excellence since 1995. Based in Chicago, Intelligentsia transformed American coffee culture and established standards for transparency, quality, and sourcing ethics that define modern specialty coffee.

**Why Intelligentsia is Legendary**:
- **Third-Wave Pioneer**: Among first to popularize light roasts, single-origins, transparency (1990s-2000s)
- **Direct Trade**: Pioneered Direct Trade model (buying directly from farmers, not exporters)
- **Transparency**: Full supply chain disclosure, farm stories, processing details
- **Competition**: Multiple World Barista Championship wins (Intelligentsia coffee used)
- **Quality**: Uncompromising quality standards, rigorous cupping protocols
- **Education**: Barista training, coffee education leader
- **Locations**: Expanded from Chicago to LA, NYC, Boston (specialty spreading)

**Historical Impact**:
- **1995**: Founded in Chicago (Lakeview neighborhood)
- **Early 2000s**: Pioneered Direct Trade (Geoff Watts traveling to origins)
- **2008-2012**: Expansion (Los Angeles, New York)
- **2015**: Acquired by Peet's Coffee (maintained independence)
- **Present**: Continues leading specialty innovation and quality

**Direct Trade Model**:
- **Personal Relationships**: Geoff Watts (green coffee buyer) builds farmer relationships
- **Farm Visits**: Annual trips, collaboration with producers
- **Fair Pricing**: Pay premiums far above commodity (quality-based)
- **Transparency**: Publish farmer names, prices, stories
- **Quality Feedback**: Help farmers improve quality through cupping feedback
- **Multi-Year**: Long-term commitments enable farm investment
- **Impact**: Model copied globally, transformed specialty sourcing

## ğŸ“‹ Overview

Espresso Macchiato is Italy's simplest milk drink - espresso "marked" (macchiato) with small dollop of milk foam, adding touch of sweetness and texture without masking espresso character. Traditional Italian afternoon coffee, perfect for those wanting espresso with slight milk softening.

**What is Macchiato**:
- **Italian**: "Macchiato" = marked/stained
- **Espresso Base**: Single or double shot
- **Foam Dollop**: Small spoonful of milk foam (1-2 tsp)
- **Minimal Milk**: Just enough to "mark" espresso
- **Small**: 2-3 oz total (espresso + foam dot)
- **Traditional**: Afternoon Italian coffee

---

## ğŸ“ Recipe

**Espresso Macchiato**:
- **Espresso**: 1-2 oz shot
- **Milk Foam**: 1-2 tsp dollop on top
- **Total**: 2-3 oz
- **Cup**: Demitasse or small cup

**Process**:
1. Pull espresso shot
2. Steam milk (create foam)
3. Spoon small dollop of foam on top of espresso
4. Serve immediately

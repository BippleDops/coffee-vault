## ğŸ¯ Steaming Technique

**Two Phases**:

**Phase 1: Stretching** (0-5 seconds):
- **Purpose**: Incorporate air, create foam
- **Position**: Wand tip just below surface
- **Sound**: Light hissing/chirping (paper tearing sound)
- **Action**: Create small vortex, introduce air
- **Result**: Volume increases 20-50%

**Phase 2: Rolling/Texturing** (5-45 seconds):
- **Purpose**: Break large bubbles, create microfoam
- **Position**: Wand deeper, create whirlpool
- **Sound**: Quiet rolling (no hissing)
- **Action**: Vortex motion, integrate bubbles
- **Result**: Glossy, paint-like microfoam

**Process** (Same as Turkish/Ibrik):
1. Cold water in briki
2. Add coffee (very fine grind)
3. Add sugar if desired
4. Stir to combine
5. Heat slowly (low-medium)

7. Remove just before boil
8. Pour (foam distributes to cups)
9. Serve immediately

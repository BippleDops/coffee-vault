## ğŸ“‹ Overview

Affogato is Italy's coffee dessert - hot espresso poured over cold vanilla gelato/ice cream, creating hot-cold contrast and coffee-cream indulgence that blurs line between coffee and dessert. Simple, elegant, delicious.

**What is Affogato**:
- **Italian**: "Affogato" = drowned (ice cream drowned in espresso)
- **Simple**: Just espresso + ice cream
- **Hot-Cold**: Temperature contrast essential
- **Dessert**: Coffee as dessert course
- **Traditional**: Italian restaurants, gelaterias

---

## ğŸ“ Recipe

**Classic Affogato**:
- **Ice Cream**: 1-2 scoops vanilla gelato/ice cream
- **Espresso**: 1-2 oz hot espresso shot
- **Glass**: Served in glass or bowl

**Process**:
1. Scoop gelato/ice cream into glass
2. Pull fresh hot espresso
3. Pour espresso over ice cream immediately
4. Serve with spoon
5. Eat quickly (melts fast)

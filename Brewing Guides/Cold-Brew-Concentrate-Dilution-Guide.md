## üìã Overview

Cold brew concentrate is cold coffee's make-ahead solution - brewing strong concentrate (1:4-1:8 ratio) that dilutes for serving, creating smooth, sweet, chocolate-forward iced coffee with zero acidity and extreme smoothness perfect for those avoiding bright coffees or making batches ahead.

**What is Cold Brew Concentrate**:
- **Concentrated**: 2-4x strength, diluted for serving
- **Cold Steep**: 12-24 hours room temp or refrigerated
- **Smooth**: No acidity, no bitterness
- **Make-Ahead**: Concentrate lasts 1-2 weeks refrigerated
- **Versatile**: Dilute to desired strength

---

## üìù Recipe

**Concentrate**:
- **Coffee**: 100g coarse ground
- **Water**: 800ml cold (1:8 ratio = concentrate)
- **Steep**: 16-20 hours
- **Result**: Strong concentrate

**Dilution**:
- **1:1**: Concentrate + water/milk/ice (standard)
- **1:2**: For milder (concentrate + 2 parts liquid)

**Process**:
1. Coarse grind coffee
2. Combine with cold water in container
3. Stir to saturate
4. Steep 12-24 hours (fridge or room temp)
5. Filter (cheesecloth, paper filter, fine strainer)
6. Store concentrate (refrigerate, lasts 1-2 weeks)
7. Dilute when serving

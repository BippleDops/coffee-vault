## ğŸ“‹ Overview

Cortado is Spain's balanced espresso-milk drink - equal parts espresso and steamed milk (little to no foam) served in small glass, creating perfect harmony where milk cuts espresso's intensity without overwhelming coffee flavor. Simple, elegant, balanced.

**What is Cortado**:
- **Spanish**: "Cortado" = cut (milk cuts espresso)
- **Ratio**: 1:1 to 1:2 espresso to milk
- **Small**: 4-6 oz total (2 oz espresso + 2-4 oz milk)
- **Steamed Milk**: Little foam (not cappuccino)
- **Glass**: Traditionally served in glass (shows layers)
- **Balance**: Milk tames espresso, coffee still shines

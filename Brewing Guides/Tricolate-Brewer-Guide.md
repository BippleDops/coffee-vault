## üìã Overview

The Tricolate is modern specialty coffee's most advanced brewer - combining immersion steeping with shower-head water distribution and extended brew times (6-10 minutes) to achieve 22-24% extraction yields that unlock sweetness and complexity impossible with standard methods.

**What Makes Tricolate Revolutionary**:
- **High Extraction**: 22-24% extraction (vs. 18-21% typical)
- **Shower Head**: Even water distribution across entire coffee bed
- **Extended Time**: 6-10 minute brews (vs. 2-4 minutes standard)
- **Immersion Component**: Coffee fully saturated throughout
- **Complexity**: Showcases absolute maximum coffee potential
- **Specialty Focus**: Designed for competition-level brewing
- **Expensive**: $200-300 (professional-grade equipment)

**Purpose**:
- Unlock maximum complexity from exceptional coffees
- Competition brewing tool
- Educational (understanding extraction limits)
- Showcase ultra-premium coffees (Geisha, competition lots)

## üìù Standard Recipe

**Parameters**:
- **Coffee**: 15-20g
- **Water**: 300g (1:15-1:20 ratio)
- **Grind**: Medium to Medium-Coarse
- **Temp**: 200-205¬∞F
- **Total Time**: 6-10 minutes (extended)
- **Target Extraction**: 22-24%

**Process**:
1. Rinse filter, add coffee
2. **Shower Distribution**: Pour water through shower head (even distribution)
3. **Steep**: Extended immersion (6-10 minutes)
4. **Draw-Down**: Slow percolation through filter
5. **Result**: Maximum extraction, ultra-complex coffee

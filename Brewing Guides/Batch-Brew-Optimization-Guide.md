## üìù Standard Recipe

**Parameters**:
- **Coffee**: 60g (for 8-cup pot, ~960ml)
- **Water**: 960ml (1:16 ratio)
- **Grind**: Medium (table salt)
- **Result**: 8 cups balanced coffee

**Process**:
1. Rinse filter
2. Add 60g ground coffee
3. Add 960ml water to reservoir
4. Start brew
5. Wait 6-8 minutes
6. Serve

## üéØ Optimization Tips

**Better Drip Coffee**:
- **Buy SCA-Certified**: Temperature matters more than any variable
- **Fresh Coffee**: 7-21 days off roast
- **Grind Fresh**: Immediately before brewing
- **Ratio**: 1:16 (60g per liter) for balanced strength
- **Clean**: Descale monthly, clean daily

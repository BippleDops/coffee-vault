### When to Use Which Filter

**V60 Filters**:
- Light roasts
- Bright, acidic coffees (Kenya, Ethiopia)
- Maximum clarity desired
- Competition (often)

**Kalita Filters**:
- Medium roasts
- Balanced coffees
- Consistency priority
- Less technique-dependent

## üìã Overview

The December Dripper is modern specialty coffee's shower-head innovation - featuring built-in shower screen distributing water evenly across entire coffee bed for uniform extraction, eliminating pouring technique variables while producing exceptionally balanced, clean coffee perfect for showcasing specialty beans.

**What Makes December Unique**:
- **Shower Head**: Built-in distribution screen (like espresso shower)
- **Even Distribution**: Water spreads uniformly across bed
- **Technique-Free**: Pour anywhere, shower distributes evenly
- **Consistent**: Removes pouring skill variable
- **Flat Bed**: Creates flat extraction bed (like Kalita)
- **Competition**: Used in competitions for consistency
- **Premium**: $80-120 (expensive but professional-grade)

**December vs. Other Pour-Overs**:
- **vs. V60**: Much more forgiving, less technique-dependent, shower distribution
- **vs. Kalita**: Similar flat bed, December has shower for even MORE consistency
- **vs. Origami**: December has shower (unique advantage)

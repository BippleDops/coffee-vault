## üéØ Basic Patterns

**Heart** (Beginner):
- **Difficulty**: Easiest pattern
- **Method**: Pour center, wiggle, cut through
- **Practice**: Learn first, foundation for others

**Rosetta** (Intermediate):
- **Difficulty**: Leaf pattern, side-to-side motion
- **Method**: Pour, wiggle while moving, cut through
- **Iconic**: Most recognizable latte art

**Tulip** (Intermediate):
- **Difficulty**: Stacked dots pattern
- **Method**: Pour dots, stack, cut through
- **Elegant**: Sophisticated appearance

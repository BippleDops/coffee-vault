## üìã Overview

Caff√® Americano is diluted espresso - espresso shots with hot water added creating drip-coffee-sized drink with espresso character. Born from American soldiers diluting Italian espresso during WWII, Americano bridges espresso intensity and American coffee portions.

**What is Americano**:
- **Diluted Espresso**: Espresso + hot water
- **Large**: 8-12 oz (drip coffee size)
- **Ratio**: Variable (1:2 to 1:6 espresso:water)
- **No Milk**: Black coffee drink
- **Espresso Character**: Maintains espresso flavor, not drip character

## üìù Recipe

**Standard Americano**:
- **Espresso**: 2 oz double shot
- **Hot Water**: 4-8 oz
- **Total**: 6-10 oz
- **Ratio**: 1:2 to 1:4

**Two Methods**:
1. **Espresso First**: Pull espresso, add water (traditional)
2. **Water First**: Add water, pull espresso on top (preserves crema)

## ğŸ“‹ Overview

**Brewing Parameters** are the controllable variables that determine extraction outcome and final cup quality. Mastery of these parameters enables precise reproduction of quality and systematic troubleshooting. Understanding parameter interactions is essential for professional brewing.

**Key Insight**: Parameters work as a system - changing one affects others. Balance is everything.

---

## ğŸ¯ The Core Parameters

**1. [[Water Temperature]]** (Brew Temp):
- **Range**: 85-100Â°C
- **Impact**: Solubility, extraction rate
- **Control**: Heating method, pre-heating
- **Effect**: Acidity, bitterness, overall extraction

**2. Grind Size**:
- **Range**: Extra-fine to extra-coarse
- **Impact**: Surface area, flow rate
- **Control**: Grinder settings
- **Effect**: Extraction rate, extraction time

**3. Brew Time** (Contact Time):
- **Range**: 2-4 minutes (typical)
- **Impact**: Total extraction opportunity
- **Control**: Manual timing, filter type
- **Effect**: Overall extraction level

**4. Water Ratio** (Coffee to Water):
- **Range**: 1:10 to 1:20 (typical)
- **Impact**: Concentration, strength
- **Control**: Measuring, consistency
- **Effect**: Body, strength, clarity

**5. Agitation**:
- **Methods**: Pour pattern, stirring, swirling
- **Impact**: Even extraction, preventing channels
- **Control**: Technique
- **Effect**: Consistency, clarity

## ğŸ“Š Systematic Adjustment

**Changing One Parameter**:
1. Identify goal (more/less acidity, etc.)
2. Select appropriate parameter
3. Make small adjustment
4. Evaluate result
5. Iterate as needed

**Example - Too Sour**:
- Increase temperature
- OR Grind finer
- OR Extend brew time
- OR Reduce agitation

**Example - Too Bitter**:
- Decrease temperature
- OR Grind coarser
- OR Shorten brew time
- OR Reduce agitation

## ğŸ¯ Method-Specific Parameters

**Pour Over (V60, Chemex)**:
- Temperature: Primary adjustment
- Grind: Flow rate control
- Time: Pour pattern determines
- Ratio: 1:15 to 1:17
- Agitation: Pour pattern critical

**Immersion (French Press, AeroPress)**:
- Temperature: Moderate-high
- Grind: Medium-coarse
- Time: 3-5 minutes
- Ratio: 1:15 to 1:18
- Agitation: Minimal (plunge timing for AeroPress)

**Espresso**:
- Temperature: Machine-controlled (93-94Â°C)
- Grind: Fine (critical)
- Time: 25-35 seconds
- Ratio: 1:2 (typical)
- Pressure: 9 bars (machine)

**Cold Brew**:
- Temperature: Room/cold
- Grind: Coarse
- Time: 12-24 hours
- Ratio: 1:8 (concentrate) or 1:15 (ready)
- Agitation: Steep only

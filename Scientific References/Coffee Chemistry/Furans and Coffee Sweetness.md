Furans are heterocyclic aromatic compounds creating coffee's sweet, caramel, roasted aromas - formed during Maillard reactions and caramelization, contributing to perceived sweetness and roast character.

**Major Furans**:
- **2-Furfurylthiol**: Primary roasted coffee aroma compound
- **Furfural**: Sweet, almond-like, caramel
- **Others**: Methyl furans, furaneol

# Modern Roasting Technology and Innovation

## Overview

Roasting technology has advanced significantly, with automation, data analytics, and environmental controls enhancing consistency, quality, and efficiency.

## Automation and Control

### PID Controllers

**Proportional-Integral-Derivative Control**:
- Automated heat management
- Maintains target curve
- Reduces human error
- Modern standard

**Benefits**:
- Extreme consistency
- Reproducible profiles
- Faster training
- Quality assurance

### Profile Automation

**Computer-Controlled Roasting**:
- Pre-programmed profiles
- Automatic execution
- Minimal operator input
- Consistency focus

**Software**:
- Artisan (open-source)
- Cropster
- Proprietary systems

## Environmental Technology

### Afterburners

**Catalytic**:
- Catalyst converts emissions
- 250-400°C operation
- Very efficient
- Lower operating cost

**Thermal**:
- High temperature oxidation (650-850°C)
- Burns all smoke/VOCs
- Very effective
- Higher operating cost

**Impact**:
- Required in many jurisdictions
- Eliminates smoke/odor
- Environmental compliance
- Neighbor-friendly

### Energy Efficiency

**Heat Recovery**:
- Capture exhaust heat
- Preheat incoming air
- Reduce fuel consumption
- 20-40% savings possible

**Modern Burners**:
- Efficient combustion
- Complete burn
- Responsive control
- Lower emissions

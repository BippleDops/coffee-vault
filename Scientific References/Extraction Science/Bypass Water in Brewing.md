## üìã Overview

Bypass water is the technique of brewing concentrated coffee then diluting with additional water to target strength - enabling higher extraction yields without over-extraction's harshness. This professional technique allows brewers to achieve 20-22% extraction (sweet spot) while controlling final brew strength independently.

**What is Bypass**:
- **DefinitionMenuBrewing concentrated (high ratio of coffee to water), then diluting
- **ExampleMenuBrew 20g coffee with 200g water (1:10) ‚Üí Add 100g water ‚Üí Final 1:15 ratio
- **PurposeMenuAchieve high extraction without over-extracting
- **BenefitMenuSweet, complex coffee at desired strength

**Why Use Bypass**:
- **Higher Extraction**: Extract more flavor (20-22%) without harshness
- **Control**: Independent control of extraction and strength
- **SweetnessMenuHigher extraction = more sweetness extracted
- **Professional**: Standard technique in specialty caf√©s
- **Competition**: World Brewers Cup competitors often use bypass
- **ConsistencyMenuEasier to hit extraction targets

**When to Use**:
- **Light RoastsMenuNeed higher extraction to develop sweetness
- **Bright CoffeesMenuExtract complexity without over-extracting acids
- **Weak Coffee**: Getting sour/under-extracted at normal ratios
- **Fine-Tuning**: Final strength adjustment after optimizing extraction

## üßÆ How Bypass Works

**The Math**:

**Scenario 1: Standard Brewing** (No Bypass):
- Coffee: 20g
- Water: 320g (1:16 ratio)
- Target Extraction: 19-20%
- **Challenge**: May under-extract (sour) or over-extract (bitter)

**Scenario 2: With Bypass**:
- Coffee: 20g
- **Brew Water**: 240g (1:12 - concentrated)
- **Bypass Water**: 80g (added after brewing)
- **Final**: 320g total (1:16 final ratio)
- **Result**: Higher extraction from concentrated brew, diluted to ideal strength

**Why It Works**:
- **Concentrated brew**: Higher coffee-to-water ratio extracts more efficiently
- **Higher TDS during brewMenuDrives extraction yield higher
- **Dilution**: Reduces final TDS to target without reducing extraction yield
- **Sweet SpotMenu 21-22% extraction at 1.35% TDS (ideal zone)

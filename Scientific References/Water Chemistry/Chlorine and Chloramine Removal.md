# üíß Chlorine and Chloramine Removal

**Category**: Water Chemistry  
**Difficulty**: Beginner  
**Purpose**: Remove chemical taste from municipal water

---

## üìã Overview

Chlorine and chloramine in municipal water create chemical off-flavors in coffee - requiring removal through carbon filtration or standing for clean-tasting brew water essential for quality coffee.

**Why Remove**:
- **Chemical Taste**: Obvious chemical/pool flavor
- **Off-Flavors**: Ruins coffee flavor
- **Essential**: First step in water improvement

**Removal Methods**:
- **Carbon Filter**: Removes chlorine, not chloramine
- **Special Filter**: Required for chloramine
- **Standing**: Chlorine evaporates (24 hours), chloramine doesn't

---

## üîß Solutions

**Activated Carbon Filter**:
- **Removes**: Chlorine effectively
- **Cost**: $20-50 (pitcher or tap filter)
- **Limitation**: Doesn't remove chloramine fully

**Catalytic Carbon**:
- **Removes**: Both chlorine AND chloramine
- **Required**: Special filter for chloramine
- **Cost**: $30-80

**RO System**:
- **Removes**: Everything (chlorine, chloramine, minerals)
- **Must Remineralize**: For coffee brewing

---

## üìù Summary

Chlorine/chloramine create chemical taste - removed through carbon filtration (chlorine) or catalytic carbon (chloramine). First essential step in water improvement for quality coffee.

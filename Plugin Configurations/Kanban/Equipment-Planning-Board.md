## How to Use This Board

### Getting Started
1. Remove the example cards (they're for reference)
2. Add your wishlist items
3. Move through research and purchase workflow
4. Track new equipment through testing
5. Document in-use equipment

### Card Format
```markdown
- [ ] Equipment Name #category #price-tier
  - Key specs
  - Price: $___
  - Status/notes
  - Link: [[Equipment Review Note]]
```

### Labels to Use
**Category**:
- `#grinder` - Burr grinders, hand grinders
- `#brewer` - V60, Chemex, French press, etc.
- `#scale` - Digital scales
- `#kettle` - Electric, stovetop, gooseneck
- `#accessories` - Filters, scoops, storage, etc.
- `#espresso` - Espresso machines and accessories

**Price Tier**:
- `#budget` - Under $50
- `#mid-range` - $50-200
- `#premium` - $200-500
- `#luxury` - $500+

**Status**:
- `#gift-idea` - Potential gift request
- `#self-purchase` - Buying for yourself
- `#shared` - Shared equipment
- `#upgrade` - Replacing existing item

**Priority**:
- `#must-have` - Essential upgrade
- `#nice-to-have` - Would improve setup
- `#luxury` - Want but not needed

### Workflow

1. **Wishlist**: Dream equipment, no immediate plans
   - Just tracking interesting items
   - No research yet
   - Someday purchases

2. **Consideration**: Active research phase
   - Reading reviews
   - Comparing options
   - Watching videos
   - Checking prices
   - Making decision

3. **Budget Approved**: Ready to buy
   - Money allocated
   - Decision made
   - Waiting for right moment (sale, payday)

4. **Purchased**: Ordered, in transit
   - Order date
   - Expected arrival
   - Tracking number
   - Excitement building!

5. **Testing**: Evaluation period (30 days recommended)
   - Learning the equipment
   - Comparing to previous gear
   - Documenting impressions
   - Deciding if keeping
   - Creating review notes

6. **In Use**: Part of regular setup
   - Proven and valued
   - Daily/weekly use
   - Maintained properly
   - Documented in vault

7. **Archived**: Retired equipment
   - Upgraded/replaced
   - Sold/donated
   - Years of service
   - Lessons learned

### Research Checklist

When a card is in **Consideration**, track:
- [ ] Read professional reviews
- [ ] Watch YouTube reviews
- [ ] Check Reddit threads
- [ ] Compare alternatives
- [ ] Check price history
- [ ] Verify compatibility with current setup
- [ ] Calculate total cost (shipping, accessories)
- [ ] Check warranty and support
- [ ] Make decision

### Testing Period Checklist

When equipment is in **Testing**, evaluate:
- [ ] Build quality and durability
- [ ] Ease of use and learning curve
- [ ] Consistency and reliability
- [ ] Cleaning and maintenance
- [ ] Impact on coffee quality
- [ ] Value for money
- [ ] Integration with workflow
- [ ] Keep or return decision

### Documentation

Create notes for significant equipment:
- Purchase details (date, price, where)
- Specifications
- Setup and settings
- Maintenance schedule
- Tips and tricks
- Pros and cons
- Links to resources
- Photos

### Linking to Vault

Link cards to:
- Equipment review notes
- Brewing guides using the equipment
- Coffee logs mentioning equipment
- Maintenance logs
- Purchase receipts (in Attachments)

### Tips for Smart Equipment Planning

**Research Phase**:
- Take your time
- Compare multiple options
- Consider long-term value
- Check for sales and deals
- Read negative reviews too

**Budget Management**:
- Set monthly equipment budget
- Track total spent
- Prioritize must-haves
- Avoid impulse purchases
- Consider used/refurbished

**Testing Strategy**:
- 30-day evaluation minimum
- Side-by-side comparisons
- Document initial vs. final impressions
- Test in various scenarios
- Decide confidently

**Maintenance Tracking**:
- Note cleaning requirements
- Track maintenance costs
- Schedule regular upkeep
- Document issues
- Keep warranty info

### Mobile Usage

This board works great on mobile:
- Quick wishlist additions
- Update testing notes
- Track deliveries
- Move cards through workflow

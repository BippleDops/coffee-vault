## Board Instructions

### Transparency Levels

**Research Needed**: No supply chain information available  
**Partial Information**: Some chain elements known, gaps exist  
**Full Transparency**: Complete chain from producer to cup  
**Investigation Priority**: High-value beans or frequent purchases needing research  
**Low Transparency**: Limited info, phasing out or reference only  
**Certification Tracking**: Verify claimed certifications

### Card Format

```markdown
- [ ] Bean Name #transparency:level #tags
  - Known: What information you have
  - Unknown: What's missing
  - Action: Steps to get more information
  - Certifications: List any certifications
  - Link: [[Bean Profile]]
```

### Transparency Scoring

- **0-3**: Low transparency (basic info only)
- **4-6**: Medium transparency (some supply chain known)
- **7-8**: High transparency (most chain documented)
- **9-10**: Complete transparency (full chain verified)

### Actions to Improve Transparency

**Contact Roaster**:
- Email for producer information
- Request lot details
- Ask about contracts and relationships

**Research Online**:
- Check roaster website
- Look for origin trip reports
- Search cooperative names

**Create Profiles**:
- Build producer profiles: [[Producers/]]
- Document supply chain
- Add to transparency dashboard

### Integration

**Supply Chain Dashboard**: [[Analytics/10-Supply-Chain-Transparency-Dashboard]]  
**Producer Profiles**: [[Producers/]]  
**Bean Profiles**: Update with producer links  

### Goals

- Target: 80% of beans with transparency score 7+
- Build: Producer relationships through purchases
- Support: Direct trade and certified coffees
- Document: Complete supply chains when available

### Best Practices

- Research before purchasing when possible
- Prioritize transparent roasters
- Support certifications (Organic, Fair Trade, Bird-Friendly)
- Build producer profiles for favorite beans
- Vote with your wallet for transparency

5. **Parchment Storage**: Until milling

## üìù Producer Story (Regional Federation History)

**Historical Context**:

Coffee arrived in Huila in early 1800s, becoming economic backbone by early 1900s. The Federaci√≥n Nacional de Cafeteros de Colombia (FNC) was established in 1927 to organize and support Colombian coffee farmers during economic crisis.

**Huila Federation Establishment** (1959):

By 1950s, Huila was emerging as major coffee region. In 1959, Huila Coffee Growers Federation was formally established as regional department of FNC, providing:
- Regional organization and representation
- Technical assistance infrastructure
- Collection and processing facilities
- Price stabilization locally

**Growth Era** (1960s-1980s):

Huila production expanded dramatically:

## üî¨ Processing Innovation (Hacienda Sonora's Hallmark)

Hacienda Sonora is internationally recognized as honey processing innovator and specialist.

**Honey Processing Mastery** (Primary Specialization):

Honey processing: pulping cherry, retaining mucilage (honey), drying with mucilage intact. Mucilage percentage and drying method create flavor variation.

1. **White Honey** (10-25% mucilage):
   - Maximum mucilage removal (mechanical demucilaging)
   - Fastest drying (7-10 days)
   - Cleanest profile, light body
   - Washed-like clarity with honey sweetness
   - Delicate, tea-like acidity

2. **Yellow Honey** (25-50% mucilage):
   - Moderate mucilage retention
   - Sun drying (10-12 days)
   - Balanced profile
   - Bright acidity, medium body
   - Honey sweetness emerging

3. **Red Honey** (50-75% mucilage):
   - Significant mucilage retention
   - Shade drying or extended sun drying (12-15 days)
   - Fuller body, deeper sweetness
   - Stone fruit notes developing
   - Caramel, brown sugar complexity

4. **Black Honey** (75-100% mucilage):
   - Maximum mucilage retention
   - Shade drying, extended period (15-20+ days)
   - Fermentation-like complexity
   - Full body, syrupy mouthfeel
   - Dark fruit, chocolate, wine notes
   - Hacienda Sonora specialty

**Traditional Processing**:

5. **Washed** (10%):
   - Full fermentation and washing
   - Clean, bright profile
   - Varietal expression clarity
   - Baseline for comparison

6. **Natural** (5%):
   - Whole cherry drying
   - Experimental lots
   - Fruit-forward profiles
   - Limited production

**Experimental Processing** (5%):

7. **Anaerobic Fermentation**:
   - Sealed tank fermentation (24-72 hours)
   - Before or during honey processing
   - Enhanced complexity and fruit notes
   - Competition lots

8. **Extended Fermentation Honey**:
   - Controlled fermentation during drying
   - Temperature and moisture management
   - Unique flavor development
   - Experimental micro-lots

9. **Carbonic Maceration**:
   - Whole cherry CO2 fermentation
   - Then honey processed
   - Layered complexity
   - Ultra-premium lots

**Processing Infrastructure**:

**Beneficio Sonora (Micromill)**:
- **Depulper**: Mechanical cherry skin removal
- **Demucilager**: Adjustable mucilage removal (honey control)
- **Drying Infrastructure**:
  - African raised beds (primary): 1,200+ square meters
  - Shade drying structures (black honey specialization)
  - Solar dryers (backup, quality control)
  - Greenhouse dryers (rain protection)
- **Moisture Monitoring**: Digital meters, continuous tracking
- **Storage**: Climate-controlled bodega (warehouse)
- **Quality Lab**: Cupping room, sample roaster, analysis equipment

**Drying Precision** (Critical for Honey):
- Constant turning (every 1-2 hours during day)
- Moisture gradient management (outside vs inside)
- Temperature monitoring (avoiding fermentation or overdrying)
- Thickness control (2-3cm layer maximum)
- Weather adaptation (moving under cover when needed)
- Final moisture target: 10.5-11.5%

**Quality Control Systems**:
- Cherry selection at harvest (ripe only)
- Floatation sorting (density separation)
- Visual defect removal
- Processing parameter documentation
- Moisture tracking throughout drying
- Pre-export cupping (every lot)
- Traceability (lot, variety, processing, harvest date)

- Direct processing control
- Quality focus and lot separation
- Direct market access

- Quality control systems refined

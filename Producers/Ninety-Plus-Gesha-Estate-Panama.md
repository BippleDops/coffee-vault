## üìù Producer Story

Ninety Plus Coffee was founded by Joseph Brodsky in 2006 with vision of treating exceptional coffee like fine wine. The name "Ninety Plus" refers to cupping scores above 90, which were rare at the time.

**The Geisha Revolution**:
Ninety Plus focused almost exclusively on Geisha variety from Panama's Boquete region, helping elevate Geisha from obscure variety to world's most sought-after coffee. Their Gesha Estate sits on volcanic slopes of Volc√°n Bar√∫ at ideal altitude (1,600-1,800m) for Geisha cultivation.

**Processing Innovation**:
Ninety Plus developed the "Level Up" processing series, experimenting with fermentation, drying, and post-harvest techniques. These innovations influenced the entire specialty coffee industry, with many producers adopting similar experimental approaches.

**Philosophy**:
"Coffee as Fine Wine" - Ninety Plus treats coffee with same attention to terroir, processing, and presentation as fine wine producers. Micro-lots are named, vintage-dated, and sold to established partners through relationship-based model.

**Legacy**:
Beyond producing exceptional coffee, Ninety Plus influenced how specialty coffee industry thinks about processing, quality, and value. They demonstrated that experimental processing could elevate already-exceptional coffee to new levels.

# Coffee Vault Business Suite

**Version**: 10.0.0 "Enterprise"
**Status**: Framework Established
**Target Users**: Coffee businesses, roasters, cafes, consultants

---

## Overview

The Business Suite extends Coffee Vault for professional use cases, providing tools for:
- Inventory management
- Financial analytics
- Customer relationship management
- Recipe costing
- Staff training
- Compliance tracking

---

## Business Templates

### Inventory Management

**File**: `Business/inventory-management-system.md`
- Stock tracking (green beans, roasted, equipment)
- Reorder alerts
- Cost analysis (COGS, waste tracking)
- FIFO/LIFO accounting
- Supplier management

### Financial Analytics

**File**: `Business/financial-analytics-dashboard.md`
- P&L statements
- ROI on equipment
- Cost per cup analysis
- Pricing optimization
- Revenue forecasting

### Customer Database (CRM)

**File**: `Business/customer-database-template.md`
- Customer preferences
- Order history
- Communication log
- Loyalty program tracking
- Roaster/shop specific features

### Recipe Costing

**Script**: `Scripts/recipe-costing-calculator.js`
- Ingredient costs
- Labor costs
- Overhead allocation
- Margin calculations
- Pricing recommendations

### Staff Training

**File**: `Business/staff-training-system.md`
- Training plans for barista teams
- Progress tracking
- Certification management (SCA, Q Grader)
- Performance evaluation
- Onboarding workflows

### Compliance Tracking

**File**: `Business/compliance-tracker.md`
- Health inspections
- Certifications (organic, fair trade, etc.)
- License renewals
- Documentation management
- Audit preparation

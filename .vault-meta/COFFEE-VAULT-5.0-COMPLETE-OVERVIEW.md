## üåü Key Features

### 1. Supply Chain Transparency

**Track from producer to cup**:
- Producer/farm profiles with certifications
- Contract and pricing details
- Sustainability metrics (environmental & social)
- Price premiums tracking
- Direct trade relationships
- Transparency scoring (1-10)

**Visualizations**:
- Supply chain map (producer ‚Üí roaster ‚Üí you)
- Transparency dashboard
- Certification tracking
- Price premium analysis

### 2. Graph Relationship System

**Interconnected data model**:
- Bidirectional relationships
- 10+ relationship types
- Semantic connections (similar-to, pairs-well-with)
- Relationship queries and traversal
- Graph visualization ready

**Relationship Types**:
- Usage: `uses-bean`, `uses-equipment`, `uses-recipe`
- Hierarchical: `parent-entity`, `child-entities`
- Semantic: `similar-to`, `influences`, `pairs-well-with`, `requires`, `enables`

### 3. Hierarchical Tagging System

**6-level tag hierarchy**:
1. **Category** - `category:coffee-log`
2. **Subcategory** - `method:v60`, `origin:ethiopia`
3. **Attributes** - `roast:light`, `processing:natural`
4. **Quality** - `quality:excellent`, `dialed-in:true`
5. **Personal** - `personal:favorite`, `to-rebuy:true`
6. **Temporal** - `month:2025-10`, `season:fall`

**Smart tag inference** auto-generates tags from properties.

### 4. Context-Aware ML

**6 advanced ML models**:

1. **Flavor Profile Predictor** - Predict flavors from bean characteristics
2. **Enhanced Parameter Optimizer** - Context-aware brewing optimization
3. **Real-Time Quality Monitor** - Live parameter validation during brewing
4. **Palate Development Tracker** - Track sensory skill growth
5. **Cost Optimization Engine** - Budget and purchasing optimization
6. **Pairing Recommender** - Optimal bean-method-equipment pairings

**Context factors**: Weather, time of day, sleep, hydration, mood, barometric pressure

### 5. Real-Time Brewing Assistance

**Live brewing guidance**:
- Real-time parameter monitoring
- Extraction calculations during brew
- Deviation alerts (when going off-track)
- Corrective action suggestions
- Statistical process control

### 6. Personal Development

**Goal tracking system**:
- Set learning goals (methods, techniques, certifications)
- Track exploration goals (origins, beans to try)
- Monitor achievement goals (ratings, milestones)
- Auto-update progress from logs
- Celebrate achievements

**Learning features**:
- Knowledge gap identification
- Personalized learning paths
- Reading progress tracking
- Skill development metrics
- Achievement badges

### 7. Recipe Management

**Reusable brewing recipes**:
- Auto-generate from successful brews
- Track recipe performance
- Recipe variations and adjustments
- Success rate monitoring
- Bean-specific recipes

### 8. Enhanced Data Capture

**Precision measurements**:
- Extraction mass and yield
- Flow rates and timing
- Temperature tracking
- Weight measurements

**Environmental context**:
- Weather conditions
- Barometric pressure
- Ambient temperature/humidity
- Lighting and noise

**Physiological context**:
- Sleep quality
- Hydration level
- Energy state
- Palate condition

**Media documentation**:
- Video links
- Photo galleries
- Audio notes
- Timelapse captures

## ğŸš§ Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge: Equipment Cost and Space**
- Home roasters range $150-1000+
- Solution: Start with affordable option (popcorn popper $30 or pan roasting free), assess interest before investing

**Challenge: Smoke and Ventilation**
- Roasting produces smoke
- Solution: Roast outdoors or ensure good ventilation, consider this in equipment/location choices

**Challenge: Green Bean Sourcing**
- Where to buy, how to evaluate, storage
- Solution: Sweet Maria's is excellent beginner source, start with sampler packs

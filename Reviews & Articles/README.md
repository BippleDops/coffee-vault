## üìÅ Folder Structure

### Equipment/
**Store**: Equipment reviews, comparisons, buying guides, manufacturer specifications

### Industry News/
**Store**: Coffee industry news, market reports, origin updates, industry developments  
**Sources**: Sprudge, Daily Coffee News, Perfect Daily Grind, trade publications  
**Format**: News clippings, market reports, analysis

### Professional Reviews/
**Store**: Professional coffee reviews, cupping notes, roaster reviews  
**Sources**: Coffee Review, roaster tasting notes, competition results  
**Format**: Cupping notes, professional reviews, competition reports

### Science & Research/
**Store**: Coffee science papers, research articles, academic studies  
**Sources**: Journal of Agricultural and Food Chemistry, research papers, SCA research  
**Format**: Academic paper PDFs/notes, research summaries, study highlights

## üîß Usage Tips

**Web Clipper Integration**:
- Use Obsidian Web Clipper or browser extension
- Save directly to appropriate subfolder
- Tag with relevant keywords (#equipment, #brewing, #science, etc.)

**Organization**:
- **Date in Filename**: YYYY-MM-DD-Article-Title.md
- **Source Attribution**: Always note source, author, date
- **Tags**: Use tags for discoverability
- **Links**: Link to related vault content (beans, origins, brewing guides)

**Best Practices**:
- Review and summarize key points
- Extract actionable insights
- Link to personal experiments/results
- Update related vault content with discoveries

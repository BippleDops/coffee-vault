China's Yunnan Province is Asia's most exciting emerging coffee origin - rapidly evolving from commodity Catimor production to specialty-focused cultivation with quality processing, experimental varieties (Geisha trials!), and growing international recognition. Yunnan coffee represents China's entry into global specialty coffee and shows remarkable potential.

**Why Yunnan Matters**:
- **China's Coffee**: 98% of Chinese coffee comes from Yunnan
- **Rapid Quality Evolution**: From commodity to specialty in 10-15 years
- **Massive Market**: Chinese domestic consumption growing explosively
- **Specialty Innovation**: Early processing experimentation (honey, natural, anaerobic)
- **Geisha Trials**: Some farms experimenting with Geisha variety
- **Altitude**: 1,000-1,800 MASL suitable for quality Arabica
- **Balanced Profile**: Chocolate-nut character, approachable, clean
- **Value**: Excellent quality at accessible pricing
- **Future**: Potential to rival established Asian origins (Indonesia, India)

## üå± Varieties

**Catimor** (Dominant, 80-90%):
- **Introduction**: 1990s mass planting (Nestl√© partnership)
- **Characteristics**: High yield, rust-resistant, lower cup quality
- **Quality**: Improving with altitude and processing
- **Status**: Still dominant but specialty farms diversifying
- **Flavor**: Nutty, woody, chocolate, neutral (better when processed well)

**Typica** (Heritage, <5%):
- **Original**: From French missionaries (1892)
- **Rare**: Low yield means minimal planting
- **Quality**: Excellent when found
- **Heritage Farms**: Some old estates maintain Typica
- **Status**: Genetic preservation, boutique lots

**Bourbon** (Limited, <5%):
- **Introduction**: Later introductions
- **Quality**: Good, sweet, balanced
- **Status**: Small specialty production

**Emerging Varieties** (Experimental, <2%):
- **Geisha**: Trial plantings in high-altitude zones (exciting potential!)
- **SL-28**: Some farms experimenting
- **Caturra**: Limited adoption
- **Future**: Yunnan's quality depends on variety transition from Catimor

## üîÑ Processing Evolution

**Washed** (Primary, 70-80%):

- Fruity, sweet profiles
- Quality improving rapidly

**Experimental** (<5%):
- **Anaerobic**: Progressive farms experimenting
- **Carbonic maceration**: Limited trials
- **Innovation**: Chinese producers eager to learn specialty techniques

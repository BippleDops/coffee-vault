# India

## Overview

**Location**: South Asia
**Regions**: Karnataka (Coorg, Chikmagalur), Kerala, Tamil Nadu
**Known For**: Monsooned coffee, shade-grown estates, unique processing

## Monsooned Coffee (Unique to India)

**Process**:
- Green beans exposed to monsoon winds
- High humidity (80%+)
- Beans swell, change color
- Unique flavor development
- 3-4 month process

**Flavor**:
- Low acidity
- Heavy body
- Earthy, spicy
- Tobacco, leather notes
- Distinctive character

**History**:
- Accidental discovery (sailing ships)
- Traditional process maintained
- Malabar region specialty
- Niche market

## Traditional Estate Coffee

**Shade-Grown Standard**:
- Multi-story cultivation
- Pepper, cardamom intercropped
- Biodiversity
- Sustainable practices

**Flavor Profile**:
- Balanced
- Spicy notes
- Medium body
- Smooth acidity

## Varieties

**Arabica**: 30% of production
- Kent, S.795, Cauvery
- Selection varieties

**Robusta**: 70% of production
- Parchment Robusta
- Bulk production
- Domestic consumption + export

## Market Position

**Monsooned**: Specialty niche
**Traditional**: Consistent quality
**Pricing**: $10-18/lb specialty
**Availability**: Good

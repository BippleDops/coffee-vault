## üìã Overview

Papua New Guinea produces exceptional specialty coffee from high-altitude volcanic highlands - smallholder-grown, cooperatively processed Arabica delivering balanced fruit-chocolate complexity with clean cup quality. While remote and infrastructure-challenged, PNG coffee represents Pacific specialty excellence with unique terroir and consistent quality.

**Why PNG Coffee Matters**:
- **High Altitude**: 1,200-2,000 MASL volcanic highlands
- **Smallholder**: 95%+ from small family gardens (1-2 hectares)
- **Cooperative Processing**: Washing stations serve communities
- **Balanced Profile**: Fruit-chocolate-nuts, complex yet approachable
- **Clean Cup**: Washed processing excellence
- **Volcanic Terroir**: Rich soil, ideal conditions
- **Underrated**: Quality exceeds international awareness
- **Sigri Estate**: Famous for exceptional quality, competition-level

**Challenges**:
- Remote, difficult logistics
- Infrastructure limitations
- Political instability affects investment
- Market access challenges
- Small scale limits volume

**Strengths**:
- High quality potential
- Unique Pacific terroir
- Strong cooperative structure
- Improving quality focus

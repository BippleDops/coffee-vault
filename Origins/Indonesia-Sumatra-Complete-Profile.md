## üìã Overview

Sumatra is Indonesian coffee's heartland - largest Indonesian Arabica producer creating signature earthy, herbal, full-bodied coffees through unique wet-hulling (Giling Basah) processing that defines Indonesian character globally. From Gayo highlands to Lintong valleys, Sumatra produces polarizing but beloved coffee perfect for those seeking extreme body without brightness.

**Why Sumatra Dominates Indonesian Coffee**:
- **Largest Arabica**: 70-80% of Indonesian Arabica from Sumatra
- **Wet-Hulling**: Giling Basah processing unique to Indonesia, perfected in Sumatra
- **Full Body**: Among world's fullest-bodied coffees
- **Low Acid**: Extremely low acidity, stomach-friendly
- **Polarizing**: Love-it-or-hate-it earthiness
- **Dark Roast Perfect**: Excels at dark roasting (no brightness to preserve)
- **French Press Icon**: Ideal for full-immersion brewing
- **Espresso**: Excellent for milk drinks (body cuts through)

**Major Sumatra Regions**:

**Aceh Province - Gayo Highlands** (North, 40-50%):
- Altitude: 1,100-1,600 MASL
- Character: Earthy-herbal, full-bodied, chocolate-spice
- Quality: Best Sumatra, specialty focus
- Organic/Fair Trade: Common certifications

**Lintong** (North, Lake Toba, 20-25%):
- Altitude: 1,100-1,600 MASL  
- Character: Classic Sumatran, herbal-chocolate
- Quality: Excellent, consistent

**Mandheling** (North Tapanuli, 15-20%):
- Altitude: 750-1,500 MASL
- Character: Full-bodied, earthy, rustic
- Name: Most famous Sumatran coffee name

**Other Regions** (10-15%):
- Sidikalang, Takengon, Aceh variations

## üîÑ Wet-Hulling (Giling Basah) Dominance

**Why Wet-Hulling in Sumatra**:
- **High Humidity**: Traditional drying impossible (mold risk)
- **No Dry SeasonMenuYear-round rainfall prevents patio drying
- **Economic**: Farmers need quick cash (can't wait weeks for drying)
- **TraditionalMenu 100+ years of practice, cultural method
- **Flavor**: Creates signature Sumatran earthiness (love it or not)

**Process**: Depulp ‚Üí Brief fermentation ‚Üí Wash ‚Üí **Partial dry (30-50% moisture)** ‚Üí **Hull while wet** ‚Üí Final drying

**ResultMenuEarthy, herbal, full-bodied, low-acid signature profile

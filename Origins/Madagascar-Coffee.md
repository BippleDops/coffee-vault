- **Island Terroir**: Isolated island evolution, unique microclimate
- **Biodiversity Hotspot**: Coffee in world's most biodiverse island
- **Bourbon Pointu**: Some Bourbon Pointu (Laurina) plantings

- **Organic**: Much production organic by default
- **Chocolate-Spice**: Unique flavor profile, island character

**Why Kivu Coffee Matters**:
- **Shares Rwanda Terroir**: Same volcanic soil, altitude, climate as Rwanda
- **High Altitude**: 1,400-2,200 MASL (ideal for quality)
- **Bourbon Variety**: Quality genetics maintained
- **Potential**: Could rival Rwanda quality if stable
- **Conflict Reality**: Decades of war prevent realization of potential

- **Social Impact**: Coffee as peace-building, economic alternative

**Challenges**:

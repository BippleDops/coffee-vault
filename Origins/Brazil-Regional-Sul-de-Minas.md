## üìã Overview

Sul de Minas is Brazil's specialty heartland - southern Minas Gerais producing more specialty coffee than any other Brazilian region through ideal altitude range (900-1,400 MASL), mixed farm sizes enabling quality focus, and perfect climate for natural processing. Sul de Minas represents Brazilian specialty's volume and quality combined.

**Why Sul de Minas Dominates**:
- **Largest Specialty Region**: More specialty coffee than anywhere else in Brazil
- **Altitude Range**: 900-1,400 MASL (ideal for quality + productivity)
- **Yellow Bourbon**: Significant Yellow Bourbon cultivation
- **Mixed Scale**: Small specialty farms + larger estates (diversity)
- **Quality + Volume**: Produces both specialty and commercial grades
- **Consistent**: Reliable quality year-over-year
- **Value**: Exceptional quality-to-price ratio

**Geography**:
- **Southern Minas Gerais**: Mountainous, rolling hills
- **Climate**: Moderate, good rainfall distribution
- **Soil**: Rich, varied (volcanic influence in some areas)
- **Harvest**: May-September (dry season, perfect for natural)

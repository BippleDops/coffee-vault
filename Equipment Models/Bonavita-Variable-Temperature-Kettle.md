## üîÑ Maintenance & Care

### Routine Maintenance

**Daily Care** (After Each Use):
- Empty kettle: Don't leave water standing (minerals settle)
- Wipe exterior: Microfiber cloth (fingerprints, spills)
- Dry interior: Tip upside down, air dry (prevent mineral buildup)
- Lid open: Store with lid open (air circulation, no musty smell)
- Time required: 1 minute

**Weekly Cleaning**:
- Interior rinse: Fill with water, swirl, dump (remove loose sediment)
- Spout rinse: Pour water through spout (clear deposits)
- Exterior wipe: Damp cloth + mild soap (clean fingerprints, oils)
- Base cleaning: Wipe base with damp cloth (dust, spills)
- Dry thoroughly: Prevent water spots
- Time required: 3-5 minutes

**Monthly Descaling** (Hard Water):
- Descaling solution: Citric acid (1-2 tablespoons per liter) or Dezcal
- Procedure:
  1. Fill kettle with descaling solution + water mix
  2. Heat to boiling (or 200¬∞F)
  3. Let sit 15-20 minutes (loosens scale)
  4. Pour out solution
  5. Rinse thoroughly (3-4 fills, boil-dump cycles)
  6. Wipe interior (remove loosened scale)
  7. Final rinse (ensure no chemical residue)
- Time required: 30-40 minutes
- Frequency: Monthly (hard water), quarterly (soft water)

**Descaling Products**:
- Citric acid: $8-$10/lb (budget option, effective)
- Urnex Dezcal: $10-$12 (commercial descaler, strong)
- White vinegar: $3-$5 (works but leaves odor, not recommended)
- Lemon juice: Natural option (less effective than citric acid)

**Descaling Procedure** (Detailed):
1. Mix solution: 1-2 tbsp citric acid per liter water
2. Fill kettle to max line (1L)
3. Heat to boiling or 200¬∞F
4. Turn off, let sit 15-20 minutes (agitate occasionally)
5. Pour out (inspect for scale chunks)
6. Rinse #1: Fill with clean water, boil, dump
7. Rinse #2: Repeat rinse cycle
8. Rinse #3: Repeat again (ensure no chemical taste)
9. Test: Boil water, taste for chemical residue (if detected, rinse again)
10. Wipe interior with cloth (remove any remaining scale)

**Storage** (Non-Use):
- Empty completely: No standing water
- Lid open: Air circulation (prevent mold, odor)
- Dry location: Room temperature (avoid humidity)
- Cord wrapped: Neat storage on base
- Duration: Indefinite (stainless steel stable)

**Hard Water Management**:
- Water filtration: Brita or similar (reduces scale buildup)
- Filtered water use: Essential in hard water areas
- Descaling frequency: Increase to monthly (or bi-weekly extreme hard water)
- Lifespan impact: Hard water = 3-year lifespan, soft water = 5+ years
- Visual check: White residue on interior = descale immediately

**Common Issues & Solutions**:
- **Slow heating**: Descale kettle (scale on element reduces efficiency)
- **Inaccurate temp**: Descale, check display vs thermometer (may need replacement)
- **Auto-shutoff early**: Scale on sensor, descale thoroughly
- **Lid sticky**: Clean hinge area, light food-safe lubricant (rare)
- **Exterior stains**: Barkeeper's Friend (stainless steel cleaner)
- **Spout clog**: Descaling solution, pipe cleaner (gentle)

**Cleaning Supplies**:
- Citric acid: $8-$10/lb (descaling, multiple uses)
- Microfiber cloths: $5-$10 (3-pack, exterior cleaning)
- Barkeeper's Friend: $5-$8 (stainless steel polish, optional)
- Dezcal: $10-$12 (commercial descaler, optional)

## üîÑ Maintenance & Care

### Routine Maintenance

**Daily Cleaning** (After Each Use):
- Backflush: If machine capable (Cafetto, water only)
- Wipe screen: Damp cloth (remove coffee residue)
- Visual inspection: Check holes not clogged
- Time: 30 seconds (same as OEM screen)

**Weekly Deep Clean**:
- Remove screen: Unscrew, carefully remove
- Soak: Cafetto Espresso Clean solution (15-20 minutes)
- Brush: Soft brush (toothbrush), gently scrub holes
- Rinse: Thoroughly (remove all chemical residue)
- Dry: Completely before reinstalling
- Reinstall: Ensure centered, tighten screw firmly
- Time: 10-15 minutes

**Monthly Descaling** (Hard Water):
- Descaling solution: Citric acid, commercial descaler
- Soak screen: 30-60 minutes (remove scale from holes)
- Brush: Gently clean after soaking
- Rinse: Thoroughly (multiple rinses)
- Inspect: Holes clear, no white residue
- Frequency: Monthly (hard water), quarterly (soft water)

**Cleaning Solutions**:
- Cafetto Espresso Clean: $12-$15 (backflush powder, weekly soak)
- Cafetto MFC Green: $10-$12 (milk system, not for screen)
- Citric acid: $8-$10/lb (descaling, monthly soak)
- Descaling solution: $10-$15 (commercial, effective)
- Water only: Daily backflush (no chemicals daily)

**Cleaning Procedure**:
1. Unscrew group head screw (center or dispersing plate)
2. Remove shower screen carefully (may be stuck to gasket)
3. Inspect gasket (replace if worn, cracked, compressed)
4. Soak screen in Cafetto solution (15-20 min, 60¬∞C water)
5. Brush gently (toothbrush, soft bristles, front and back)
6. Rinse thoroughly (running water, ensure chemical removal)
7. Dry completely (paper towel or air dry)
8. Clean group head (brush, wipe, remove coffee oils)
9. Reinstall screen (centered, flat against gasket)
10. Tighten screw (firm but not overtightened, can crack screen)

**Inspection Checklist**:
- Holes clear: No coffee grounds, scale, or oil buildup
- Flatness: Screen not warped or bent
- Edge condition: No chips, cracks, damage
- Surface: Mirror polish intact (no corrosion, pitting)
- Gasket: Screen seals evenly (no leaks around edge)

**Replacement Indicators**:
- Flow rate slows: Despite cleaning, flow degraded
- Scale buildup: Can't clean holes clear
- Warping: Screen no longer flat (rare but possible)
- Damage: Chips, cracks, corrosion (very rare with stainless)
- Lifespan: 2-5 years typical (replace proactively if performance drops)

**Gasket Replacement**:
- Frequency: Every 6-12 months (independent of screen)
- Cost: $5-$10 (OEM gaskets)
- Compatibility: IMS screen works with OEM gaskets
- Installation: Replace gasket when installing/removing screen
- Signs needed: Coffee leaking around portafilter, loose lock

**Storage** (If Removed):
- Dry completely: Prevent corrosion (stainless resistant but not immune)
- Protective case: Ziplock bag, small box (prevent bending)
- Location: Dry, room temperature (avoid humidity)
- Duration: Indefinite (stainless steel stable)

**Hard Water Management**:
- Water filtration: Essential (Brita minimum, RO ideal)
- Third Wave Water: Optimizes water for espresso and scale prevention
- Descaling frequency: Increase to monthly (hard water areas)
- Machine descaling: Regular schedule prevents screen clogging
- Lifespan: Accept 2-year replacement (hard water reality)

**Common Issues & Solutions**:
- **Slow flow**: Descale screen, check for clogged holes
- **Leaking portafilter**: Replace gasket, check screen flat
- **Uneven extraction**: Ensure screen centered, not warped
- **Channeling returns**: Check puck prep, grind quality (not screen fault)
- **Bitter shots**: May need coarser grind (slower flow = longer extraction)
- **Screen won't sit flush**: Check group head cleanliness, gasket condition

**Tools Needed**:
- Screwdriver: Flathead or Phillips (depending on machine)
- Soft brush: Toothbrush or group head brush ($8-$15)
- Cleaning solution: Cafetto Espresso Clean ($12-$15)
- Descaler: Citric acid or commercial ($8-$15)
- Microfiber cloth: Wiping, drying
- Container: For soaking screen (small bowl, cup)

**Maintenance Cost** (Annual):
- Cleaning solution: $15-$20/year
- Descaler: $10-$15/year (hard water)
- Gasket replacement: $5-$10/year
- Screen replacement: $15/year (amortized over 3 years)
- **Total annual cost**: $45-$60/year (all group head maintenance)

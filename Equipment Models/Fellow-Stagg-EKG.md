## üí≠ Personal Assessment

### Overall Impression

The Fellow Stagg EKG is the most beautiful electric kettle on the market. It's also one of the most functional for pour-over brewing. The precision gooseneck provides exceptional pour control. Temperature accuracy is excellent. The hold mode is genuinely convenient. Build quality inspires confidence. Using it is a daily aesthetic pleasure.

However, the $195 price is steep for a kettle. The Bonavita performs 90% as well for $75. The OXO is adequate for $100. You're paying a significant premium for Fellow's design excellence and build quality. Whether that premium is justified depends on how much you value aesthetics and the joy of using beautiful objects.

For pour-over enthusiasts who appreciate design, the Stagg EKG is worth every penny. It makes daily brewing more pleasurable. It elevates your coffee station aesthetically. It provides professional-grade temperature control and pour precision. It's a tool you'll use every day and enjoy using.

For budget-conscious users or those ambivalent about design, the Bonavita or Brewista offers better value. The Stagg's benefits are real but subtle‚Äîbetter pour control, better aesthetics, better build. If these matter to you, the Stagg is worth it. If not, save $120 and get the Bonavita.

### Strengths

**Design Excellence**: Simply the most beautiful electric kettle available. Clean lines, perfect proportions, premium materials. It's a countertop sculpture that makes coffee.

**Pour Control**: The precision gooseneck provides exceptional control. Steady, controlled streams. Perfect for pour-over techniques requiring precision pouring.

**Temperature Precision**: ¬±1¬∞F accuracy. Dial to exact temperature needed. Hold mode maintains temp for 60 minutes. Professional-grade control in home kettle.

**Build Quality**: 304 stainless steel body, quality powder coat, solid construction. Feels premium, built to last.

**Balanced Ergonomics**: Weighted handle provides perfect balance. Comfortable to hold and pour even when full.

**Ease of Use**: Simple interface. Dial to temp, press button, done. No complex menus or settings (standard EKG).

### Weaknesses

**Price**: $195 is expensive for electric kettle. Bonavita is $75, OXO is $100. You're paying significant premium for design.

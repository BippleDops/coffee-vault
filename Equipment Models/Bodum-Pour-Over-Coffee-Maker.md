## üî¨ Technical Notes

### Optimal Brewing Technique

**Recommended Recipe** (full 34 oz pot):
1. **Grind**: 60-70g coffee, medium-coarse (French press-like)
2. **Boil water**: 1 liter (34 oz) water, bring to boil, let cool 30 seconds (~200¬∞F)
3. **Rinse filter**: Pour hot water through empty filter (preheat carafe, rinse filter)
4. **Add grounds**: Place 60-70g coffee in permanent filter
5. **Bloom** (optional): Pour 100-150ml water, wait 30 seconds (bloom)
6. **Pour**: Slow circular pour, saturate all grounds evenly
7. **Brew time**: 3-4 minutes total (gravity drip)
8. **Serve**: Pour into cups, leaving sediment at bottom of carafe
9. **Cleanup**: Discard grounds, rinse filter and carafe immediately

**Grind Size**:
- **Too coarse**: Fast drip (< 3 minutes), under-extraction, weak/sour coffee
- **Optimal**: Medium-coarse (French press-like), 3-4 minute drip
- **Too fine**: Slow drip (> 5 minutes), over-extraction, bitter coffee + excessive sediment

**Coffee-to-Water Ratio**:
- 1:15 ratio (stronger): 70g coffee per 1 liter water
- 1:16 ratio (balanced): 60g coffee per 1 liter water
- 1:17 ratio (lighter): 55g coffee per 1 liter water

### Common Mistakes

**Grinding Too Fine**:
- **Issue**: Clogs filter, excessive sediment, over-extraction, bitter coffee
- **Solution**: Use medium-coarse grind (French press-like)

**Not Cleaning Filter**:
- **Issue**: Coffee oils accumulate, go rancid, create off-flavors in subsequent brews
- **Solution**: Rinse filter after each use, dishwasher weekly

**Pouring Too Fast**:
- **Issue**: Uneven saturation, channeling, uneven extraction
- **Solution**: Slow circular pour (similar to V60 technique, though less critical)

**Expecting V60-Like Clarity**:
- **Issue**: Permanent mesh filter allows oils/fines through (not defect, by design)
- **Solution**: Accept trade-off (full body vs clarity) or insert paper filter into mesh

### Maintenance

**After Each Use**:
- Discard coffee grounds from filter
- Rinse filter under hot water (remove grounds/oils)
- Rinse carafe
- Air dry or towel dry

**Weekly** (if used daily):
- Dishwasher clean (filter, carafe, lid)
- Deep clean filter mesh (remove accumulated oils)

**Monthly**:
- Soak filter in Cafiza solution (remove rancid oils)
- Descale carafe if hard water residue visible
- Inspect filter mesh for damage (rarely needed)

**Troubleshooting**:
- **Coffee tastes oily/rancid**: Clean filter thoroughly (oils have gone rancid)
- **Too much sediment**: Grind coarser, clean filter mesh
- **Weak coffee**: Grind finer (but not too fine, or sediment increases)
- **Bitter coffee**: Grind coarser, reduce brew time, lower water temperature

3. **Display cleaning**: Soft cloth on display (anti-reflective coating)
4. **Bottom cleaning**: Clean rubber feet (prevent slip, maintain levelness)
5. **Inspection**: Check for any damage or wear

**Monthly Maintenance**:
1. **Deep clean**: Slightly damp cloth with mild soap
2. **Port inspection**: Check charging port for debris
3. **Cable check**: Inspect charging cable for damage
4. **Accuracy check**: Test with known weight (e.g., 500g)
5. **Battery health**: Full charge/discharge cycle (maintain battery)
6. **Firmware check**: Open app, check for updates
7. **Calibration check**: If accuracy seems off, consider re-calibration

**Storage**:
- **Short-term (daily)**: Leave on counter, dust cover optional
- **Medium-term (weeks)**: Store in dry location, 50% battery charge
- **Long-term (months)**: Clean thoroughly, 50% charge, cool dry place
- **Avoid**: Direct sunlight, extreme temperatures, high humidity
- **Protection**: Original box ideal for long-term storage

**Battery Care**:
- **Regular charging**: Charge when battery reaches 20-30%
- **Avoid deep discharge**: Don't let battery fully drain regularly
- **Periodic full cycle**: Once per month, full discharge ‚Üí full charge
- **Storage charge**: 50% charge for long-term storage
- **Temperature**: Charge at room temperature (avoid extreme cold/heat)
- **Replacement**: After 500+ cycles (2-3 years), battery holds less charge
- **Replacement service**: Contact Acaia for battery replacement ($50-70)

**Troubleshooting**:

**Scale won't power on**:
- Check battery charge (may be fully depleted)
- Plug in to charger for 15 minutes, try again
- Ensure long-pressing power button (1 second hold)
- Try hard reset: charge for 30 minutes, then power on

**Inaccurate readings**:
- Ensure scale on level, stable surface
- Tare scale with nothing on it
- Check for debris under scale or on weighing surface
- Allow temperature stabilization (15 minutes)
- Perform user calibration with 500g weight
- Check battery level (low battery affects accuracy)

**Timer not auto-starting**:
- Verify auto-start enabled in app settings
- Check auto-start sensitivity (may be too low)
- Ensure sufficient weight increase (>0.5g minimum)
- Try manual timer start instead
- App connection may be required (check Bluetooth)

**Bluetooth won't connect**:
- Enable Bluetooth on phone
- Ensure scale powered on
- Enter pairing mode: hold all 3 buttons 3 seconds
- Forget device in phone settings, re-pair
- Close and re-open Acaia app
- Update app to latest version
- Restart phone
- Try different phone (rule out phone issue)

**Display too dim/bright**:
- Auto-brightness adjusting to ambient light
- Manually adjust in app settings
- Clean display (dust/oil reduces brightness)
- Check battery level (low battery dims display)

**"OVER" error**:
- Weight exceeds 2000g capacity
- Remove excess weight immediately
- Scale locks until weight removed
- No damage occurs (protection feature)

**Erratic readings**:
- Surface vibration (move to stable surface)
- Air currents (close windows, move away from fan)
- Electromagnetic interference (move away from induction, microwave)
- Debris under scale (clean bottom and surface)
- Damaged load cell (contact support)

**Scale auto-powers off**:
- Auto-off feature active (configurable in app)
- Default: 5 minutes of inactivity
- Change setting: app ‚Üí settings ‚Üí auto-off ‚Üí select time
- Option: set to "never" for continuous use (drains battery faster)

**Charging issues**:
- Check cable connection (fully inserted)
- Try different USB adapter or computer USB
- Try different cable (cable may be damaged)
- Clean charging port (use compressed air)
- Battery may be end-of-life (2-3 years, needs replacement)

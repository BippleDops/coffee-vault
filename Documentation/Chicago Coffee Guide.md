## ðŸ’¡ Chicago Coffee Hacks

### Insider Tips

**Free Coffee**:
- Cuppings at Gaslight (free tastings)
- Cupping events at roasteries (usually free)
- Coffee shop "buy 10 get 1 free" cards

**Best Deals**:
- Subscribe & Save (10-15% off recurring)
- Wholesale access (if you know someone)
- Day-old pastry + coffee deals
- Refill discounts (bring your cup back)

**Avoid**:
- Airport coffee (overpriced, stale)
- Tourist trap Loop locations
- Hotel coffee (bring your own beans!)

**Peak Hours to Avoid**:
- 7-9am weekday (commuter rush)
- 12-1pm lunch crowds
- Weekend brunch (10am-1pm)

**Best Times**:
- 6-7am (quiet, focused)
- 2-4pm (afternoon lull, baristas have time to chat)
- After 6pm (evening crowd, relaxed)

### Chicago-Specific Challenges

**Winter**:
- Beans can get cold in transit/car - bring inside immediately
- Very dry air - store beans carefully
- CafÃ© crowding - arrive early for seats

**Summer**:
- Heat can affect bean storage - keep cool and dry
- Iced drinks premium priced
- Outdoor seating competitive

**Lake Effect**:
- High humidity certain days - can affect grinding and extraction
- Monitor and adjust grind as needed

**What is Direct Trade**:
- **Direct Relationships**: Roaster buys directly from farmer/cooperative
- **No Middlemen**: Bypasses exporters, importers (or minimal involvement)
- **Farm Visits**: Roasters visit farms, build personal relationships
- **Quality Focus**: Quality-based pricing, feedback loops
- **Multi-Year**: Long-term commitments enable farm investment
- **Transparency**: Full supply chain disclosure

**Pioneers**:
- **Intelligentsia**: Coined "Direct Trade," pioneered model (early 2000s)
- **Counter Culture**: "Direct Trade" parallel development
- **Sustainable Harvest**: "Relationship Coffee" (similar concept, 1990s)

## ğŸ’° Economics

**Farmer Benefits**:
- **Better Prices**: $6-20/lb vs. commodity $1-3/lb
- **Stability**: Multi-year contracts, predictable income
- **Quality Premiums**: Rewarded for quality improvements
- **Investment**: Higher income enables farm improvements

**Roaster Benefits**:
- **Quality**: Access to exceptional lots before general market
- **Consistency**: Same farms year-over-year, quality consistency
- **Story**: Farm stories, transparency, marketing value
- **Differentiation**: Unique coffees, competitive advantage

**Challenges**:
- **Scale**: Only viable for small-medium roasters (large roasters need volume)
- **Logistics**: Complex shipping, import/export
- **Risk**: Weather, political instability affects supply
- **Investment**: Time and money for farm visits

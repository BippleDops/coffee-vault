## üí∞ Revenue Streams

**Espresso Drinks** (60-70% revenue):
- **Margins**: 70-85% (highest)
- **Average**: $4-6 per drink
- **Volume**: 100-300 drinks/day

**Drip Coffee** (10-15%):
- **Margins**: 80-90%
- **Average**: $2-4
- **Volume**: 20-100 cups/day

**Retail Beans** (10-15%):
- **Margins**: 40-60%
- **Average**: $15-20/bag
- **Volume**: 5-30 bags/day

**Food** (10-20% if offered):
- **Margins**: 50-70%
- **Complexity**: Increases operations

## üìà Path to Profitability

**Startup** (Months 1-6):
- **Revenue**: $10,000-30,000/month
- **Costs**: $25,000-45,000/month
- **Status**: Losing money (expected)

**Growth** (Months 7-18):
- **Revenue**: $30,000-60,000/month
- **Costs**: $28,000-50,000/month
- **Status**: Approaching breakeven

**Established** (18+ months):
- **Revenue**: $50,000-100,000/month
- **Costs**: $35,000-70,000/month
- **Profit**: $10,000-30,000/month (20-30% margins)

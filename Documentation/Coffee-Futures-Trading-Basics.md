## ğŸ“‹ Overview

Coffee futures trading (ICE exchange, C-Market) enables price hedging and speculation - farmers/traders locking in prices months ahead while speculators provide market liquidity, creating volatile commodity market affecting millions of farmers globally.

**What are Coffee Futures**:
- **Futures Contract**: Agreement to buy/sell coffee at future date, set price
- **ICE**: Intercontinental Exchange (New York), primary coffee futures market
- **C-Market**: Arabica coffee futures ("C Contract")
- **Hedging**: Farmers/buyers lock in prices (risk management)
- **Speculation**: Traders bet on price movements (provide liquidity)

**Why Futures Matter**:
- **Price Discovery**: Market determines coffee value
- **Risk Management**: Hedging protects against price swings
- **Volatility**: Prices fluctuate dramatically (weather, politics, speculation)
- **Farmer Impact**: C-Market sets floor for commodity coffee

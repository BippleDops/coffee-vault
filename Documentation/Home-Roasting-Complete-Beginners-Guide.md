## üìã Overview

Home coffee roasting enables fresh-roasted coffee at significant savings ($4-8/lb vs. $15-25/lb retail) while providing control over roast level, variety selection, and ultimate freshness for passionate coffee enthusiasts willing to invest time and equipment.

**Why Home Roast**:
- **Freshness**: Maximum freshness (roast, rest, consume within weeks)
- **Cost**: $4-8/lb green vs. $15-25/lb roasted (50-70% savings)
- **Control**: Choose varieties, roast levels, profiles
- **Learning**: Understand roasting, deepen coffee knowledge
- **Fun**: Engaging hobby, experimentation

**Investment**:
- **Equipment**: $50-2,000 (method-dependent)
- **Time**: 30-60 minutes per roast (including cooling, cleaning)
- **Learning Curve**: Dozens of roasts to master
- **Space**: Ventilation essential (smoke)

## üî• Home Roasting Methods

**Popcorn Popper** ($20-40, Entry-Level):
- **Method**: Hot air fluid bed, unmodified popper
- **Capacity**: 100-150g batches
- **Pros**: Cheapest entry, easy, fast (5-8 min roasts)
- **Cons**: Limited control, small batches, short lifespan
- **Best For**: Trying home roasting, ultra-budget

**Heat Gun + Bread Bowl** ($40-80, DIY):
- **Method**: Heat gun, metal bowl/bread maker drum
- **Capacity**: 200-400g
- **Pros**: Cheap, decent control, larger batches
- **Cons**: Manual, requires attention, smoky
- **Best For**: DIY enthusiasts, budget-conscious

**Behmor 1600 Plus** ($350-400, Popular Home Roaster):
- **Method**: Drum roaster, electric
- **Capacity**: 300-500g batches
- **Pros**: Affordable drum roaster, smoke reduction, decent control
- **Cons**: Learning curve, limited profile control
- **Best For**: Serious home roasters, apartment-suitable

**Fresh Roast SR** ($200-250, Fluid Bed):
- **Method**: Fluid bed, electric
- **Capacity**: 100-150g batches
- **Pros**: Easy, clean, fast, consistent
- **Cons**: Small batches, limited dark roast capability
- **Best For**: Light roast focus, small households

**Hottop** ($1,200-1,500, Prosumer):
- **Method**: Drum roaster, programmable
- **Capacity**: 250-300g
- **Pros**: Excellent control, programmable profiles, consistent
- **Cons**: Expensive, small batches
- **Best For**: Serious hobbyists, quality obsessed

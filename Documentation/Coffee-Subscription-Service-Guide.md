Coffee subscriptions provide recurring coffee delivery - enabling exploration of global coffees, roaster discovery, and convenience while supporting roasters through predictable recurring revenue.

**What are Coffee Subscriptions**:
- **Recurring**: Weekly, biweekly, or monthly deliveries
- **Curated**: Roasters select coffees (exploration)
- **Convenience**: Automatic delivery, never run out
- **Discovery**: Try coffees you wouldn't choose
- **Support**: Recurring revenue helps roasters

**Types**:
1. **Single Roaster**: Subscribe to favorite roaster (consistency)
2. **Multi-Roaster**: Services featuring different roasters (variety)
3. **Specialty Clubs**: Curated exceptional coffees (premium)

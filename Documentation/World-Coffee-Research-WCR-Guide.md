## üìã Overview

World Coffee Research (WCR) is coffee's global research organization - developing climate-resilient varieties (F1 Hybrids), coordinating international research, and addressing industry challenges through science, ensuring coffee's sustainable future.

**What is WCR**:
- **Research Organization**: Global coffee research coordination
- **Variety Development**: F1 Hybrids (Centroamericano, Starmaya, others)
- **Climate Adaptation**: Breeding for changing conditions
- **Disease Resistance**: Developing rust-resistant quality varieties
- **Industry-Funded**: Roasters, producers fund research
- **Collaboration**: Coordinates research across institutions globally

**Why WCR Matters**:
- **F1 Hybrids**: Created varieties combining quality + resistance + climate resilience
- **Future**: Coffee's sustainable future depends on variety innovation
- **Coordination**: Prevents redundant research, shares knowledge
- **Farmer Support**: New varieties enable sustainable farming
- **Climate**: Essential for coffee adaptation to warming

## üå± F1 Hybrid Program

**Revolutionary Achievement**:
- **Quality + Resistance**: First varieties achieving both
- **Centroamericano, Starmaya**: Flagship varieties
- **85-88+ Points**: Competition-level cup quality
- **Disease Resistant**: Rust, berry disease, nematodes
- **Climate Resilient**: Performs under stress
- **Productive**: 30-50% higher yield than Bourbon

**Impact**:
- Proves breeding can surpass traditional varieties
- Enables sustainable specialty coffee future
- Farmers adopting rapidly (quality + economics)

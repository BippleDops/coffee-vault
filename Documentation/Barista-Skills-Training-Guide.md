## üìã Overview

Professional barista skills span espresso extraction, milk steaming, latte art, customer service, equipment maintenance, and coffee knowledge - developed through practice, training, and experience creating career opportunities in specialty coffee.

**Core Barista Skills**:
1. **Espresso**: Dialing in, extraction, consistency
2. **Milk Steaming**: Microfoam perfection, temperature control
3. **Latte Art**: Pour patterns, presentation
4. **Workflow**: Speed, efficiency, multi-tasking
5. **Customer Service**: Interaction, hospitality, sales
6. **Coffee Knowledge**: Origins, brewing, tasting
7. **Equipment**: Maintenance, troubleshooting, care

---

## ‚≠ê Skill Development Path

**Beginner** (0-6 months):
- **Espresso Basics**: Pull acceptable shots, understand variables
- **Milk Steaming**: Create acceptable microfoam
- **Drinks**: Make standard drinks (latte, cappuccino)
- **Customer Service**: Basic interaction, POS operation
- **Cleaning**: Proper equipment cleaning protocols

**Intermediate** (6-18 months):
- **Dialing In**: Optimize espresso for different coffees
- **Microfoam**: Consistent velvety texture
- **Latte Art**: Basic patterns (heart, tulip)
- **Speed**: 30-40 drinks/hour during rush
- **Knowledge**: Origin basics, processing, brewing methods

**Advanced** (18+ months):
- **Competition Level**: Championship-caliber espresso
- **Advanced Latte Art**: Rosettas, swans, complex patterns
- **Troubleshooting**: Diagnose and fix equipment issues
- **Training**: Teach others, develop team
- **Expertise**: Deep coffee knowledge, cupping, quality assessment

- **Barista Championships**: National, regional, world levels
- **Latte Art Throwdowns**: Local competitions, skill building
- **Brewers Cup**: Manual brewing competition

**Career Paths**:

- **Caf√© Manager**: Operations, staff, business

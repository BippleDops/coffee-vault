Bird-Friendly certification (Smithsonian Migratory Bird Center) is strictest shade-grown standard - requiring 40%+ canopy cover, 10+ native tree species, 3 canopy layers, AND organic certification, ensuring maximum biodiversity habitat for migratory birds.

**Why Strictest**:
- **Shade**: 40%+ canopy (higher than others)
- **Diversity**: 10+ native tree species required
- **Structure**: 3 distinct canopy layers (forest structure)
- **Organic**: Must also be organic certified
- **Habitat**: Proven migratory bird habitat

**Migratory Birds**:
- **2/3 of US migratory birds** winter in coffee regions
- **Habitat Loss**: Coffee monoculture destroys habitat
- **Bird-Friendly**: Preserves forest habitat in coffee farms

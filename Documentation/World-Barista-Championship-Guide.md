## ğŸ“‹ Overview

World Barista Championship (WBC) is specialty coffee's premier barista competition - national champions competing globally through 15-minute routines preparing espresso, milk drinks, and signature beverages judged on technical skill, taste, and presentation.

**What is WBC**:
- **Annual**: Held annually (since 2000)
- **National Champions**: Each country sends champion
- **15-Minute Routine**: Prepare 4 espresso, 4 milk drinks, 4 signature drinks
- **Judges**: Technical judges (technique) + sensory judges (taste)
- **Scoring**: Technical skill, taste, presentation, creativity
- **Prestige**: Winning WBC is career-defining

**Format**:
1. **National Championships**: Each country holds nationals
2. **WBC**: National champions compete globally
3. **Semifinals**: Top competitors advance
4. **Finals**: Best 6 compete for championship

## ğŸ¯ Competition Requirements

**4 Espresso Shots**: Judges evaluate straight espresso  
**4 Milk Drinks**: Cappuccinos or lattes, milk texture critical  
**4 Signature Drinks**: Creative beverages (alcohol-free, espresso-based)

**Judging Criteria**:
- **Technical**: Espresso extraction, milk steaming, cleanliness
- **Taste**: Espresso quality, milk drink balance, signature creativity
- **Presentation**: Stage presence, communication, professionalism

**Time**: 15 minutes (setup + preparation + service)

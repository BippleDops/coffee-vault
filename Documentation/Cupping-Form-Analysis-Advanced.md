## üìã Overview

Advanced cupping form analysis extracts maximum information from scores - revealing quality patterns, processing effects, roast appropriateness, and value assessment through systematic score interpretation beyond simple total points.

**What to Analyze**:
- **Attribute Patterns**: Which scores high/low (reveals character)
- **Balance**: Score distribution (balanced vs. extreme)
- **Processing**: Washed vs. natural score patterns
- **Value**: Quality relative to price
- **Defects**: Types and severity

---

## üìä Score Pattern Interpretation

**High Acidity + High Sweetness** (8-9 points each):
- **Profile**: Kenyan, Ethiopian, high-altitude washed
- **Quality**: Exceptional balance, complex
- **Value**: Premium justified

**High Body + Low Acidity** (Body 8-9, Acidity 6-7):
- **Profile**: Brazilian, Indonesian, natural process
- **Quality**: Full-bodied, smooth
- **Style**: Different excellence, not inferior

**Balanced Across All** (7-8 points all attributes):
- **Profile**: Colombian, Guatemalan, balanced coffees
- **Quality**: Versatile, broadly appealing
- **Value**: Excellent for daily drinking

**Unbalanced** (Some 9s, some 6s):
- **Profile**: Distinctive, polarizing (Kenya extremes)
- **Quality**: Can be excellent but selective appeal

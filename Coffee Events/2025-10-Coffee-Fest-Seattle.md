## üìù Event Notes

**General Observations**:

Seattle Coffee Fest 2025 was an incredible experience - the energy, passion, and quality on display was inspiring. The event brought together the entire specialty coffee community from producers to roasters to baristas to enthusiasts.

**Key Learnings**:

1. **SCA Cupping Protocol**: Learned the formal cupping protocol from certified Q Graders. Practiced with 8 different coffees - eye-opening how systematic evaluation reveals nuances.

2. **Climate Change Impact**: Origin panel discussed serious challenges - rising temperatures, changing rainfall, altitude shifts. Many producers adapting through shade trees and drought-resistant varieties.

3. **Processing Innovation**: Saw incredible experimental processing - anaerobic fermentation, carbonic maceration, extended fermentation. The flavor profiles were wild and unprecedented.

4. **Equipment Advances**: Latest grinder technology is impressive - even particle distribution is getting better. Saw new features in espresso machines (pressure profiling becoming standard).

5. **Community Passion**: Met so many passionate coffee people. Everyone willing to share knowledge and experiences. The community is incredibly welcoming.

**Insights**:

- Quality is reaching new heights every year
- Transparency and sustainability are becoming industry standard
- Direct trade relationships are growing
- Technology is enabling better brewing at home
- The gap between professional and home brewing is closing

**Follow-Up Actions**:
- Connect with Sarah Chen about their sourcing trips
- Try the SCA cupping protocol at home
- Research Panama Gesha more deeply
- Practice formal sensory evaluation
- Plan to attend workshops throughout the year
- Consider Q Grader certification in future
